# Marvelous Creations — Brand Voice

> Entity: ENT-05 | Voice guidelines for Arena Habitat SPV communications.

---

## Voice Principles

MC communications are **operational, precise, and governance-aware**. Every output should reflect the discipline expected of an SPV managing investor capital.

### Tone
- **Professional and factual** — no hype, no speculation
- **Numbers-first** — always lead with data (EUR amounts, percentages, timelines)
- **Governance-conscious** — flag thresholds, approvals, and compliance requirements proactively
- **Concise** — shorter is better; stakeholders are busy

### Do
- State amounts in EUR with thousands separator (500,000 EUR)
- Reference governance tiers when discussing expenditure
- Distinguish GP (Realization) vs LP (Zodiaco) responsibilities
- Use "Arena Habitat" not "the project" on first reference
- Include timeline context (phase, milestone, deadline)

### Do Not
- Use marketing language or emotional appeals
- Make projections without stating assumptions
- Use em dashes — use commas or periods instead
- Use superlatives (amazing, incredible, revolutionary)
- Conflate MC with Realization or Burtucala — MC is a separate legal entity

### Audience
- Primary: GP and LP stakeholders reviewing project status
- Secondary: Contractors, architects, and municipal authorities
- All communications should be suitable for formal record-keeping

---

## Examples

**Good:** "Phase 2 electrical work: 3,200 EUR. Budget line B-04, within GP authority (under 5,000 EUR threshold). Contractor: Silva Electricidade."

**Bad:** "We've got an amazing electrician who's going to do incredible work on the wiring!"
