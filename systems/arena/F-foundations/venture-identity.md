## The Project: Arena

**Concept:** "Missing Middle" housing. Quality residential units for the under-35 demographic at price points between social housing and luxury development.

Arena exemplifies the Realization ecosystem's Urban Infill philosophy — healing urban fabric by creating quality housing within existing urban areas rather than expanding into greenfield land.

**Location:** Barreiro, Portugal (south bank of the Tagus, improving transport links, growing demand)

**Scale:** 11 residential units (A through K)

**Total sellable area:** 578.42 sqm

**Construction cost:** 1,450/sqm

**Total revenue target:** 2,646,474

**Timeline:** 21-month project duration

**Bank:** NovoBanco

## Iron Principles

These three principles govern all Escorpiao operations. They are non-negotiable.

### 1. Zero Budget Creep
No euro leaves the project without a budget line and prior control. Every expense must trace to the approved budget. Unbudgeted items require formal approval before commitment.

### 2. Radical Transparency
All project information is available to all stakeholders at all times. Document storage (Google Drive), project management (ClickUp), and financial data are accessible. No information silos.

### 3. Separation of Powers
The executor (Asaf/Meirav) is NOT the budget approver (Roy). No single person can both commit and approve expenditure. This prevents conflicts of interest and ensures financial discipline.

### 4. Worst-Case First
No investor materials or pitch narratives are created until the financial model's catastrophe scenario has been validated. The math must show that even in the worst case, the land value protects the principal. Truth Engine before Trust Engine.
