## Joint Decision Axis

All financial decisions follow this approval matrix:

### Tier 1: < 5,000
- **Process:** Internal GP approval
- **Who approves:** Asaf or Roy (unilateral)
- **Timeline:** Same day
- **Documentation:** ClickUp task + budget line reference

### Tier 2: 5,000 - 10,000
- **Process:** Joint GP decision
- **Who approves:** Asaf AND Roy (both must agree)
- **Timeline:** Within 48 hours
- **Documentation:** ClickUp task + budget line reference + both approvals documented

### Tier 3: > 10,000
- **Process:** LP approval required
- **Who approves:** Eldad (Zodiaco Badalado)
- **Timeline:** Within 5 business days
- **Documentation:** Formal request via email + ClickUp + budget line reference + LP written approval

### Variance Trigger: > 2.5% of Total Budget
- **Process:** Mandatory LP notification
- **Who notifies:** Roy Hadar
- **Timeline:** Within 48 hours of variance identification
- **Documentation:** Written notification with variance explanation, cause analysis, and remediation plan

## Decision Domains

### GP Exclusive Decisions (No LP Approval Needed)
- Day-to-day project management
- Contractor coordination and scheduling
- Technical design decisions within approved scope
- Marketing tactics and content
- Team internal processes

### Joint GP/LP Decisions (LP Approval Required)
- Budget amendments > 10,000 or > 2.5% variance
- Major contractor selection (general contractor, key trades)
- Changes to project scope or unit count
- Sales pricing strategy
- Capital calls or distributions
- Legal structure changes

### LP Reserved Rights
- Approval of annual budget
- Approval of capital calls
- Approval of exit strategy and timing
- Right to audit all financial records
- Right to replace GP team members (with cause)

## Conflict Resolution

1. **GP internal disagreements:** Resolved in GP Weekly Sync. If unresolved, escalate to LP.
2. **GP-LP disagreements:** Formal discussion with written positions. LP has final authority on reserved matters.
3. **Partner-GP disagreements:** Mediated by the GP member who owns the partner relationship.
4. **Financial disputes:** Roy's budget assessment is the starting point. Disputed items escalated per Joint Decision Axis.

## Amendment Process

Changes to this governance charter require:
1. Written proposal by any GP member
2. GP consensus (all three pillars)
3. LP approval (Eldad)
