## Regular Meetings

### GP Weekly Sync
- **Frequency:** Weekly (Monday)
- **Attendees:** Asaf, Roy, Meirav
- **Duration:** 30-45 minutes
- **Format:** Video call (Zoom/Google Meet)
- **Purpose:** Coordination across pillars, decision-making, blocker resolution

**Standing Agenda:**
1. Last week: key activities completed (each pillar, 3 min)
2. This week: priorities and commitments (each pillar, 3 min)
3. Blockers: anything stuck or delayed (5 min)
4. Decisions needed: items requiring discussion (10 min)
5. Financial pulse: cash position, upcoming payments (Roy, 3 min)
6. Action items: who, what, by when

---

### Design Review
- **Frequency:** Bi-weekly
- **Attendees:** Asaf + SFRG (Arc. Ramiro Guinote)
- **Duration:** 60-90 minutes
- **Format:** Video or in-person (varies)
- **Purpose:** Architectural progress review, design decisions, regulatory alignment

**Typical Agenda:**
1. Design progress since last review
2. Drawings review (current stage)
3. Technical issues or constraints
4. Regulatory requirements update
5. Decisions needed
6. Next deliverables and timeline

### Legal Check-in
- **Frequency:** Monthly
- **Attendees:** Asaf, Meirav, Adv. Tania Duarte

- **Purpose:** Legal matters, contracts, regulatory compliance

4. Sales legal (CPCV status, buyer issues)

- **Purpose:** Accounting, tax compliance, financial reporting

4. Tax compliance updates

### LP Monthly Update
- **Frequency:** Monthly (by 15th of following month)
- **Attendees:** GP team (Asaf, Roy, Meirav) + Eldad
- **Duration:** 45-60 minutes
- **Format:** Zoom call
- **Purpose:** Monthly Management Report review, Q&A, decisions

**Typical Agenda:**
1. Report walkthrough (Roy presents financials, Asaf presents technical, Meirav presents sales)
2. Key risks and mitigations
3. LP questions and discussion
4. Decisions requiring LP approval
5. Next month outlook

**Pre-work:** Monthly Management Report sent 3-5 days before the call.

---

### Quarterly Strategic Review
- **Frequency:** Quarterly
- **Attendees:** GP team + Eldad
- **Duration:** 90-120 minutes
- **Format:** Video or in-person (preferred for quarterly)
- **Purpose:** Strategic assessment, phase progress, forward planning

**Typical Agenda:**
1. Quarter summary (achievements, challenges)
2. Phase progress (where we are vs. plan)
3. Financial health (budget, cash, forecast)
4. Sales outlook (pipeline, market conditions)
5. Risk assessment (updated register)
6. Strategic questions for discussion
7. Next quarter plan and priorities
