### Access Rules
- All GP members: full access to all folders
- LP (Eldad): read access to all folders

### File Naming Convention

Example: `2026-02-15_Legal_ContractorAgreement_GeneralContractor.pdf`

## Project Management: ClickUp

### 3 Spaces

| Space | Purpose | Owner |
|-------|---------|-------|
| Planning & Permitting | Licensing, design reviews, construction milestones | Asaf |
| Finance & Legal | Budget tracking, payments, legal tasks | Roy |
| Sales & CRM | Buyer pipeline, agency management, marketing tasks | Meirav |

### Task Structure
- Every task has: owner, due date, priority, status
- Statuses: To Do, In Progress, Waiting, Done
- Tags: Urgent, LP Approval, Budget Impact, Milestone

### Usage Rules
- All tasks created in ClickUp (not just discussed verbally)
- Status updated minimum weekly
- Overdue tasks reviewed in GP Weekly Sync
- Financial tasks always tagged with budget line reference
