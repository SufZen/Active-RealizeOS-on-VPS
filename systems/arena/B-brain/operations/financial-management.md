## Budget Control

### Master Excel
- **Document:** Interactive Business Plan v4
- **Location:** Google Drive (02_Financial)
- **Sole editor:** Roy Hadar (Finance Controller)
- **Structure:** Line-item budget by category with monthly actuals

### Budget Rules
1. Every expense must have a corresponding budget line
2. No budget line? Request an amendment before spending
3. Budget amendments follow the governance matrix (< 5K, 5-10K, > 10K)
4. Contingency is a budget line, not a blank check. Contingency usage is tracked separately.
5. Monthly Plan vs. Actual comparison is mandatory

### Variance Management
- Track at line-item level monthly
- Aggregate variance tracked against total budget
- Trigger: > 2.5% of total budget = mandatory LP notification
- Every variance > 5% at line-item level gets an explanation in the monthly report

## VAT Management

### VAT Rates Applicable
| Category | VAT Rate | Notes |
|----------|----------|-------|
| New construction | 6% | Reduced rate for new residential construction |
| Professional services | 23% | Architecture, legal, accounting, consulting |
| Equipment and materials | 23% | Standard rate (some exceptions) |
| Administrative costs | 23% | Office, travel, communication |

### VAT Recovery
- Input VAT on construction costs is recoverable
- Monthly VAT returns submitted by Audax
- Track recoverable VAT separately in cash flow forecast
- VAT recovery timing: typically 30-60 days after submission

### VAT Compliance
- All invoices must include correct VAT rate
- NIF (tax number) verification for all vendors
- Monthly reconciliation of VAT charged vs. VAT paid
- Annual VAT reconciliation with tax authorities
