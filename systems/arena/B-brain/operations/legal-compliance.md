## CPCV (Pre-Sale Contract) Framework

### What is a CPCV?
Contrato de Promessa de Compra e Venda (CPCV) is a pre-sale agreement binding both buyer and seller to complete the transaction. It is standard practice in Portuguese real estate.

### CPCV Key Terms
- **Parties:** Escorpiao LDA (seller) + Buyer
- **Subject:** Specific unit (identified by letter A-K, floor, sqm)
- **Price:** Agreed sale price (base 4,200/sqm, adjusted per unit specs)
- **Deposit:** Typically 10-20% of sale price (sinal)
- **Completion date:** Linked to construction completion + Form 4
- **Conditions:** Subject to obtaining Form 4, clear title, no encumbrances
- **Penalties:** Buyer default = forfeit deposit. Seller default = return deposit x 2 (per Portuguese law)

### CPCV Process
1. Buyer qualification (Meirav)
2. Unit selection and price agreement (Meirav + GP consensus)
3. Draft CPCV (Meirav prepares, Tania reviews)
4. Buyer review and negotiation
5. CPCV signing
6. Deposit collection (Roy processes)
7. Track completion conditions
8. Schedule escritura (final deed) when Form 4 obtained

### Legal Review
- Every CPCV reviewed by Adv. Tania Duarte before signing
- Any non-standard terms flagged to GP team
- Deposit handling compliant with Portuguese consumer protection law

## Contractor Agreements

### Standard Contractor Agreement Terms
- **Scope:** Clearly defined with specifications and drawings referenced
- **Price:** Fixed-price preferred. Unit rates for variable work.
- **Payment terms:** Progress-based. Retention: 5-10%. Final payment after inspection.
- **Timeline:** Milestone-based with completion date
- **Change orders:** Written only. Budget impact assessed by Roy before approval.
- **Insurance:** Contractor must maintain liability and workers' compensation insurance
- **Warranties:** Minimum 5-year structural, 2-year MEP, 1-year finishes
- **Termination:** For cause with 30-day cure period. For convenience with fair compensation.
- **Dispute resolution:** Mediation first, then Portuguese courts
