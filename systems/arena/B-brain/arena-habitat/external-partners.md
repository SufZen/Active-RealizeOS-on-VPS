## Partner Engagement Rules

1. **Written agreement first.** No verbal-only engagements.
2. **Scope before price.** Define deliverables before negotiating fees.
3. **SOP 1 compliance.** All partner engagements follow the Procurement SOP.
4. **Governance matrix.** Engagement > 10K requires LP approval.
5. **Single GP owner.** Every partner has one primary GP contact. Escalation goes through that contact.
6. **Performance tracking.** All partners tracked against deliverables and timelines.
