# Arena Habitat — Development Plan

> Entity: ENT-05 | 4-phase macro timeline. Q1 2026 to Q4 2028.

---

## Macro Timeline

**Total estimated duration:** 30 months (2.5 years)

```
Phase 1: Acquisition & Setup     Q1-Q2 2026     (6 months)
Phase 2: Licensing & Planning    Q2 2026-Q1 2027 (9 months)
Phase 3: Construction            Q1 2027-Q3 2028 (18 months)
Phase 4: Handover & Exit         Q4 2028         (3 months)
```

---

## Phase 1: Acquisition & Setup (Q1-Q2 2026)

**Duration:** ~6 months
**Capital source:** Seed equity (265,000)

### Key Activities
- GP team assembly and role definition
- SPV setup and governance framework
- Land acquisition finalization
- Initial architect engagement (SFRG)
- PIP (Pedido de Informacao Previa) submission
- Seed equity deployment
- Tech stack setup (ClickUp, Google Drive, communication channels)
- External partner onboarding (legal, accounting)

### Key Milestones
- [ ] GP team roles confirmed and documented
- [ ] Land acquisition complete
- [ ] PIP submitted to Camara Municipal de Barreiro
- [ ] Google Drive Data Room structured
- [ ] ClickUp spaces created
- [ ] External partners (Tania, Audax) engaged

### Risks
- PIP response delay (Camara processing times vary)
- Land acquisition complications (title, encumbrances)
- Team coordination during setup phase

---

## Phase 2: Licensing & Detailed Planning (Q2 2026 - Q1 2027)

**Duration:** ~9 months
**Capital source:** Seed equity (remaining) + pre-construction costs

### Key Activities
- Detailed architectural design (with SFRG)
- Specialty engineering coordination (structural, MEP)
- Building permit application
- Construction tender preparation and execution
- General contractor selection
- Series A activation (if needed)
- NovoBanco construction loan finalization
- Marketing strategy development
- 3D visualization and sales materials production

### Key Milestones
- [ ] Detailed design complete
- [ ] Building permit submitted
- [ ] Building permit approved
- [ ] Construction tender issued
- [ ] General contractor selected
- [ ] NovoBanco loan agreement signed
- [ ] Sales materials ready

### Risks
- Building permit delays (most common risk in Portuguese development)
- Contractor pricing above budget (market conditions)
- NovoBanco loan conditions or timeline
- Design changes during permit review

---

## Phase 3: Construction (Q1 2027 - Q3 2028)

**Duration:** ~18 months
**Capital source:** Construction loan (NovoBanco draw-downs)

### Key Activities
- Site preparation and foundation
- Structural construction
- MEP installation
- Interior finishing
- Landscaping and common areas
- Fiscalizacao oversight throughout
- Progress-based loan draw-downs
- Sales campaign (begin pre-sales when legally permitted)
- Monthly quality inspections

### Key Milestones
- [ ] Foundation complete
- [ ] Structure topped out
- [ ] MEP rough-in complete
- [ ] Interior finishing underway
- [ ] First units ready for inspection
- [ ] All units construction complete
- [ ] Pre-sale CPCVs signed (target: majority before completion)

### Risks
- Construction delays (weather, labor, materials, supply chain)
- Cost overruns (material price increases, change orders)
- Quality issues (require rework, impacting timeline and budget)
- Subcontractor coordination
- Sales pace below projections

---

## Phase 4: Handover & Exit (Q4 2028)

**Duration:** ~3 months
**Capital source:** Sales proceeds

### Key Activities
- Final inspection and Form 4 (Utilizacao license)
- Defect identification and resolution (snag list)
- Unit handover to buyers
- Final CPCV closings / escrituras
- Construction loan repayment (from sales proceeds)
- Capital waterfall distribution
- Project close-out documentation
- Lessons learned and potential replication analysis

### Key Milestones
- [ ] Form 4 obtained (all units)
- [ ] All defects resolved
- [ ] All units handed over to buyers
- [ ] Construction loan repaid
- [ ] Capital distributed per waterfall
- [ ] Project formally closed

### Risks
- Form 4 delays (inspection scheduling, minor non-compliance)
- Buyer default on final payment
- Outstanding defects requiring additional contractor engagement

## Timeline Dependencies

| Dependency | Impact If Delayed |
|-----------|------------------|
| PIP response | Delays detailed design start |
| Building permit | Delays construction start (single biggest risk) |
| NovoBanco loan approval | Delays construction funding |
| General contractor selection | Delays construction start |
| Form 4 | Delays handover and sales completion |
