## Quick Reference — Tiered Decision Matrix

| Threshold | Approval | Timeline | Documentation |
|-----------|----------|----------|---------------|
| Operational (<5,000) | GP (Asaf) unilateral | Same day | ClickUp task, reported monthly |
| Significant (5,000-15,000) | GP proposes, LP has 48h right of refusal | 48 hours | ClickUp + written proposal to LP |

## Fundamental Decisions (Always Unanimous)

These decisions always require agreement from both GP and LP, regardless of amount:

- Dissolution or winding up of the SPV
- New equity issuance or ownership dilution
- New debt beyond approved capital stack
- Sale of land or project assets (outside normal unit sales)
- Change of project scope (units, location, use)
- Admission of new shareholders
- Related-party transactions
- Capital calls for new investment rounds

## Operational Examples

### Tier 1: Operational (<5,000) — GP Unilateral

**Examples:**
- Office supplies and admin tools
- Small professional consultations
- Site visit expenses
- Minor equipment purchases
- Routine accounting fees
- Small marketing expenses

**Process:**
1. Requestor identifies need
2. Roy confirms budget line exists
3. Asaf approves
4. Logged in ClickUp
5. Reported to LP in monthly financial report

---

### Tier 2: Significant (5,000-15,000) — GP Proposes, LP Right of Refusal

**Examples:**
- Specialist engineering reports
- Marketing campaign production
- Professional fee installments
- Equipment for site preparation
- Legal retainer payments

**Process:**
1. GP documents need with budget justification
2. Roy confirms budget line and available funds
3. Written proposal sent to LP (email)
4. LP has 48 hours to refuse (silence = consent)
5. If refused: GP revises or escalates to joint discussion
6. Logged in ClickUp with LP notification record

---

### Tier 3: Strategic (>15,000 or >5% Variance) — Joint Approval

**Examples:**
- General contractor engagement
- Major architectural fee payments
- Land-related expenses
- Large equipment or material orders
- Construction milestone payments
- Any cost overrun exceeding 5% of total budget

**Process:**
1. GP team prepares formal request
2. Includes: amount, purpose, budget line, alternatives considered, timeline impact
3. Submitted to LP (Eldad) via email
4. LP has 5 business days to respond
5. Written approval required before commitment
6. Logged in ClickUp with LP approval attached

## Conflict Resolution

1. **Good faith negotiation:** 15 days between GP and LP
2. **Independent mediator:** Appointed within 10 days if negotiation fails. Costs shared equally.
3. **Portuguese courts:** Comarca de Setubal if mediation fails
4. **Financial disputes:** Roy's budget assessment is the starting point for any financial disagreement

---

## Deadlock Mechanism

If GP and LP cannot agree on a strategic or fundamental decision within 30 days:
1. Independent mediator appointed (agreed upon or court-appointed)
2. Mediation within 15 days
3. If unresolved: either party may propose a buyout of the other's quota at fair market value (independent valuation)
