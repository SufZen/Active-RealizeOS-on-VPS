## 4. How Money Comes Back — The Waterfall

When units are sold, proceeds are distributed in this strict order. **No one gets paid at a later step until all earlier steps are fully satisfied.**

### Step 1 — Return of Capital (Last In, First Out)

| Priority | Payment | Amount |
|----------|---------|--------|
| 1st | Repay bank loan (NovoBanco) | ~770,000 + 5% interest |
| 2nd | Return Round 4 debt + 16% interest | 296,000 + interest |
| 3rd | Return Round 3 debt + 18% interest | 436,000 + interest |
| 4th | Return Round 2 debt + 20% interest | 380,000 + interest |
| 5th | Return Round 1 equity (seed capital) | 265,000 |
| 6th | Return Developer sweat equity | ~46,000 |

### Step 2 — Preferred Returns

| Priority | Payment | Amount |
|----------|---------|--------|
| 7th | 10% annual preferred return on Round 1 | On 265,000 |

### Step 3 — Profit Split (Promote)

| Priority | Payment | Split |
|----------|---------|-------|
| 8th | Remaining profit | **70% to LP (Zodiaco)** / 30% to GP (Realization) |

**What this means for Eldad/Zodiaco:** Your 250,000 investment is returned in full before any profit is split. You then receive a 10% preferred return on top. Only after that does any profit get divided — and even then, you receive 70% of the upside. Expected multiplier: **1.45x** (21.6% annual IRR).

## 5. Governance — Who Decides What

### 5.1 Decision Thresholds

| Decision Size | Who Approves | Response Time |
|--------------|-------------|---------------|
| Under 5,000 | GP (Asaf) alone | Same day — reported to LP monthly |
| 5,000 to 15,000 | GP proposes, LP can refuse | 48 hours |
| Over 15,000 (or >5% budget deviation) | Both must agree in writing | 5 business days |
| Fundamental decisions (see below) | Both must agree (unanimous) | As needed |

### 5.2 Fundamental Decisions (Always Require Both Partners)

- Dissolving the company
- Issuing new equity or diluting ownership
- Taking on debt beyond the approved capital stack
- Selling the land or project (outside normal unit sales)
- Changing project scope (number of units, location, use)
- Admitting new shareholders
- Any related-party transactions

### 5.3 Banking Controls

- **Dual signature required** for all bank transactions above **15,000**
- Single signature (GP) for day-to-day operations under 15,000
- Monthly bank statements provided to LP with financial report

## 6. Manager Rights & Safeguards

### Current Structure
Asaf serves as sole manager (gerente) — this is standard for an SPV where the GP runs the project.

### LP Protections

**Manager replacement:** Under Portuguese law, a 75% vote can replace the manager. As 67% holder, the LP can trigger a replacement vote. Specific triggers that constitute "cause":

- Fraud or misappropriation of funds
- Gross negligence in project management
- Abandonment of responsibilities (more than 30 consecutive days without justified cause)
- Material breach of the shareholders' agreement
- Failure to provide required financial reports for 2+ consecutive months

**Key-person clause:** If Asaf is unable to continue (health, force majeure, or any other reason):
- LP has 60 days to appoint a replacement manager
- If no replacement is found, either party can trigger orderly dissolution
- GP's promote rights are preserved for work completed up to that date

## 7. What Happens If We Stop — Exit by Project Phase

| Phase | If GP Wants Out | If LP Wants Out | If Both Agree to Stop |
|-------|----------------|-----------------|----------------------|
| **Phase 1: Before land purchase** | Capital returned pro-rata. Costs shared proportionally. | Same as GP exit. | Same — clean exit. |
| **Phase 2: After land, before construction** | LP keeps the land and full SPV. GP forfeits promote. | LP receives land value (independent valuation) minus GP's documented sweat equity. | Land sold, proceeds distributed per waterfall. |
| **Phase 3: During construction** | **No unilateral exit.** Must complete the project. Non-performing party loses promote/upside. | **No unilateral exit.** Same rules apply. | Orderly wind-down. Assets sold. Waterfall applies. |
| **Phase 4: After construction** | Standard waterfall on sales proceeds. | Standard waterfall on sales proceeds. | Standard waterfall. |

**Key principle:** At every phase, the LP's capital is protected first. The waterfall ensures money comes back to investors before any profit is split.

## 10. Dispute Resolution

If we disagree on something:

1. **Good faith negotiation** — 15 days to work it out between us
2. **Independent mediator** — appointed within 10 days if we can't agree. Costs shared equally.
3. **Courts** — Comarca de Setubal, only as last resort if mediation fails

## 11. Other Protections

- **Tag-along:** If either partner gets a buyout offer, the other partner can sell on the same terms
- **Drag-along:** If the 67%+ holder gets a bona fide offer for the entire SPV, they can require the minority to sell — but only if the price satisfies the full waterfall (all capital + preferred returns covered)
- **Non-compete:** Neither partner develops competing residential projects in Barreiro during the project + 12 months after
- **Confidentiality:** All financial and commercial terms are confidential for 3 years after the project ends
