## Capital Waterfall (Distribution Priority)

At exit (sale of units), proceeds flow in this strict order:

### Step 1 — Return of Capital (LIFO)
1. Repay bank loan (NovoBanco) — ~770,000 + interest
2. Return Round 4 capital + 16% interest
3. Return Round 3 capital + 18% interest
4. Return Round 2 capital + 20% interest
5. Return Round 1 capital (265,000 principal)
6. Return Developer sweat equity (~46,000)

### Step 2 — Preferred Returns
7. Pay Round 1 preferred return (10% annual on 265,000)

### Step 3 — Promote / Upside Split
8. Remaining profit:
   - **30% to GP (Realization)** — Developer Promote
   - **70% to LP (Zodiaco Badalado)** — distributed pro-rata to Round 1 investors

## Financial Controls

- All expenditure must trace to a budget line in the Master Excel
- Variance > 5% triggers mandatory LP notification and joint approval
- Payment approval follows Tiered Decision Matrix (see governance-matrix.md)
- Monthly Plan vs. Actual report to LP
- Cash flow forecast maintained rolling 3-6 months
- Dual-signature for bank transactions above 10,000
