# Arena — Project Overview

> Entity: ENT-04 (project) / ENT-05 (SPV) | 11-unit residential development, Barreiro

---

## The Concept: Missing Middle

Arena targets the "Missing Middle" of Portuguese housing: quality residential units at price points between social housing and luxury development, designed for the under-35 demographic.

**Why it matters:** Young professionals in the greater Lisbon area are priced out of central Lisbon but underserved by suburban options. Arena bridges this gap with well-designed, efficiently built units in an improving area with good transport links.

## Location: Barreiro

**Why Barreiro:**
- South bank of the Tagus, opposite Lisbon
- Improving transport infrastructure (ferry, future rail connections)
- Significantly more affordable than Lisbon proper
- Growing demand from young professionals commuting to Lisbon
- Municipal investment in area improvement
- Less saturated than comparable areas on the north bank

---

## Target Buyer Profile

- Age: 25-35
- Occupation: Young professionals, couples, first-time buyers
- Income: Mid-range (sufficient for mortgage qualification)
- Priorities: Quality design, good location, transport access, value for money
- Not looking for: Luxury finishes, large sqm, prestige addresses
- **Tax catalyst:** Under-35 buyers may qualify for IMT exemption on properties below 316,772
