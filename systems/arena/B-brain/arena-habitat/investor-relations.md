## Communication Rules

### Transparency
- No information surprises. LP should hear news from GP first, not from the market.
- Bad news travels fast. If something goes wrong, notify LP within 48 hours.
- Financial data is always current. Monthly reconciliation is non-negotiable.

### Tone
- Professional, concise, factual
- Present problems with solutions (not just problems)
- Use numbers, not adjectives
- Acknowledge uncertainties honestly

### Channels
- **Formal:** Email for reports, memos, approval requests
- **Informal:** WhatsApp ("Arena Strategic" group) for quick updates, scheduling
- **Meetings:** Zoom for monthly reviews, in-person for quarterly strategic reviews when possible

### Approval Requests
When requesting LP approval (strategic decisions per governance matrix):
1. Written request with full context
2. Budget impact analysis
3. Recommendation with reasoning
4. Timeline for decision needed
5. Allow 5 business days for response

## Capital Call Protocol

1. **Identification:** Finance Controller identifies capital need for next round
2. **Assessment:** GP team confirms necessity, timing, and terms (per approved capital stack)
3. **Preparation:** Capital & Sales prepares formal request with:
   - Amount requested and round number
   - Purpose and budget line
   - Timeline for deployment
   - Current project status
   - Impact of not calling (alternatives considered)
4. **Notice:** 30 days written notice to investors
5. **Approval:** LP approval required (fundamental decision)
6. **Cure period:** 15 days for any shortfall
7. **Execution:** Upon approval and funding, capital deployed per plan
