# Realization AI Adoption — Service One-Pager

## What we sell

**Custom AI adoption for solopreneurs, small businesses, and small teams (≤20 people) — done structurally, hands-on, with results that ship.**

Not "another AI marketing consultant." Most AI consulting is talk: someone tells the operator what to do with ChatGPT, they nod, nothing changes. We are different on three axes:

1. **Structural, not tactical.** We start with how the business actually works — workflows, decisions, bottlenecks, software stack — and design AI in *around* the structure, not bolted on top.
2. **Hands-on, not advisory.** We can build it with the client, or build it for them. The scope flexes; the deliverable always ships.
3. **Venture architect + relationship-driven service in one team.** Asaf brings 15+ years of venture building (Realization studio, RealizeOS, multi-region operations). Meirav brings client management, sales, marketing, and customer success depth. The engagement is high-context from minute one.

## Roles

- **Asaf** = Business Architect & Delivery Lead — structural delivery, runs discovery sessions, designs roadmaps, executes implementation
- **Meirav** = Client Manager & Service Lead — sales, marketing, customer success, owns the funnel from first touch through ongoing relationship

## ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)

### Primary segments

| Segment | Profile | Common pain |
|---|---|---|
| Solo professionals & small clinics (1–5 people) | Therapists, coaches, functional medicine, course creators, podcasters | Decision fatigue, content paralysis, scattered tools, no funnel |
| Real estate agencies (3–15 people) | Boutique residential or commercial brokers | Lead management chaos, listing workflow, missed follow-ups, content production |
| Architecture firms (3–20 people) | Small/medium architectural studios | Project documentation, proposal generation, knowledge management, client comms |
| Small RE developers (2–10 people) | 1–5 simultaneous projects | Project documentation, investor reporting, contractor coord, deal underwriting |
| Service agencies (5–20 people) | Marketing, design, branding, consulting | Workflow automation, proposals/SOWs, project mgmt, content production |

### Common pattern across all segments
- 1–20 people
- Running on intuition or fragile spreadsheets
- Tried Monday/Asana/HubSpot, didn't stick
- Owns at least one paid AI tool, uses 5% of capability
- Coordination via WhatsApp groups
- Wants to use AI but doesn't know how to do it right
- Decision fatigue, partner coordination chaos
- Can absorb €30/mo SaaS easily, willing to invest €1,000s for a real foundation

### Disqualifiers
- ❌ Companies > 20 people (different sales motion)
- ❌ "Help me prompt ChatGPT" expectations
- ❌ Cheap freelance work seekers
- ❌ Heavily regulated industries we're unfamiliar with (banking, insurance, healthcare med-devices)
