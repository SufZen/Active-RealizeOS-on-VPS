# Creations

This directory stores all deliverables produced by the AI team.

Files are saved here automatically when agents complete work:
- Blog posts, articles, social media content
- Reports, analyses, research summaries
- Proposals, pitch documents
- Email drafts, newsletter content
- Any other output

## Naming Convention

Files are named descriptively with dates:
- `blog-post-topic-name-2024-01-15.md`
- `competitor-analysis-q1-2024.md`
- `email-campaign-launch-draft.md`

## Tip

Review this folder periodically to see what your AI team has produced. Move finalized content to your publishing platform or archive older pieces.
