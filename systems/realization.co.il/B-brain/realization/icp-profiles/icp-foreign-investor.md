# ICP: "David" — Foreign Investor / Post-Exit Entrepreneur

The primary Ideal Customer Profile for Realization's services.

---

## Who Is David

- **Age:** Mid-40s
- **Profile:** Tech entrepreneur (successfully exited) or sophisticated private investor
- **Operates:** Internationally
- **Standards:** Exceptionally high — both in business and in aesthetics
- **Mindset:** Legacy-driven; wants ventures and assets that outlast him

## What David Wants

- To build a **legacy** — profitable masterpieces that reflect his personal values and aesthetic taste
- His ventures to be both **financially sound** and **beautifully crafted**
- To maintain **personal freedom** — staying out of the daily management weeds
- A trusted partner who can manifest his vision perfectly (e.g., a luxury development in Portugal)

## What's Blocking David

- **Informational noise** — too many options, too many "experts," not enough clarity
- **No partner who shares his obsession with quality** — hard to find someone who truly gets it
- **The strategy-soul gap** — he can't find someone who understands both the cold logic of an ROI model AND the soul of a beautifully designed space

## Where David Lives (Digitally & Physically)

- International tech and investment circles
- High-end real estate and design communities
- Selective about content — values depth over volume
- Likely on LinkedIn, curated Telegram groups, invite-only networks
- Attends premium events, not mass conferences

## How to Reach David

### Content Strategy
- **Thought leadership** that demonstrates the strategy + aesthetics combination
- **Case studies** (walk-through stories) that show real projects
- **Data-driven analysis** with beautiful presentation
- **Personal narrative** that builds trust over time

### Messaging Triggers
- "ROI model and architectural soul built as one" — this resonates
- Contrast frames: "Most see an abandoned building / I see a portfolio of untapped potential"
- Specific numbers anchored in reality (not hype)
- The bridge metaphor (connecting what David has to what David wants)

### What Turns David Off
- Hype, urgency, scarcity tactics → instant credibility loss
- Generic "we're the best" claims → he's heard this a thousand times
- Oversimplified pitches → he's sophisticated enough to see through them
- Aggressive CTAs → he decides on his own terms

---

## David's Decision Process

1. **Discovery:** Encounters content that demonstrates real expertise
2. **Evaluation:** Reads multiple pieces — tests for consistency and depth
3. **Connection:** Reaches out when convinced this person *gets it*
4. **Due diligence:** Wants to see real projects, real numbers, real references
5. **Partnership:** Commits when trust is established — then he's loyal
