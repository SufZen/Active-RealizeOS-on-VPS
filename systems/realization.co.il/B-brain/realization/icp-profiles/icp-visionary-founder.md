# ICP: Visionary Founder — Launch, Pivot, or Scale

Secondary Ideal Customer Profile for Realization's Brand & Creative Realization pillar.

---

## Who They Are

- **Profile:** Founders or firms wanting to launch, pivot, or scale with clarity and creative impact
- **Appreciate:** Strong aesthetics and thoughtful brand identity
- **Need:** Robust digital presence and effective audience engagement
- **Mindset:** They know what they want to feel like, but can't articulate or execute it alone

## What They Want

- A brand identity that reflects their vision — not a template
- Strategic clarity on positioning, messaging, and market entry
- Digital presence that actually converts (not just looks pretty)
- Someone who understands both the creative vision AND the business model

## What's Blocking Them

- Most branding agencies do surface-level work — pretty but hollow
- Most strategy consultants do dry frameworks — logical but soulless
- Can't find someone who bridges both worlds

## How Realization Serves Them

Through the **Brand & Creative Realization** pillar:
- Brand naming, storytelling, and positioning
- Content strategy and creative direction
- Community-building and digital engagement
- SEO and performance marketing optimization
- Web presence through Wix, AI tools, and automations
