## What Realization Is

A **Strategic Venture Studio and Consultancy** — the overarching entity through which Asaf Eyzenkot operates all business activities. It is both:
- **Umbrella:** Launches, supports, and provides strategic services to all ventures
- **Direct service provider:** Consultancy services to external clients

## Three Pillars

### Pillar 1: Space & Real Estate
- Revitalization of abandoned and underutilized properties
- Real estate investment analysis
- Rental optimization (short-term and long-term)
- On-site project management
- Property scouting and deal structuring
- Architectural design and renovation supervision
- Focus on 8%+ CoC return

### Pillar 2: Business Strategy & Innovation
- Building new ventures or reshaping existing ones
- Strategic planning and investment modeling
- Workflow automation and digital tools (Make.com, AI, Google Sheets)
- Venture co-creation and advisory
- Strategic partnerships and market expansion
- Tax and legal structure guidance (high-level, connecting to experts)

### Pillar 3: Brand & Creative Realization
- Brand naming, storytelling, and positioning
- Content strategy and creative direction
- Community-building and digital engagement
- SEO and performance marketing optimization
- Web presence (Wix, AI tools, automations)

## Key Differentiators

1. Operates across **physical and digital** domains simultaneously
2. Combines **local boots-on-the-ground** insight with **high-level strategic** thinking
3. Leverages **design, data, and automation** for elegant, efficient solutions
4. Deeply rooted in **personal trust, aesthetic quality, and strategic clarity**
5. Focus on **long-term value** creation, not short-term gain
6. The **"Inside-Out" approach**: rare hybrid of deep-thinking strategist + uncompromising aesthetic artist + AI-augmented execution

## Revenue Model

### Direct Services
- Single Property Purchase Consultation (fee-based, scaled by property value)
- Construction Project Consultation & Supervision (monthly/weekly/hourly)
- Preliminary/Monthly Consultation Packages (fixed rate, paid in advance)
- Real estate mediation commissions (percentage of transaction)

### Venture Revenue
- Revenue from ventures in the portfolio (Burtucala, HomeAid, Second Base, etc.)
- Architectural project fees through Marvelous Creations LDA

### Infrastructure
- AMI License for real estate mediation
- Civil Liability Insurance (Allianz)
- Registered as Realization Unipessoal LDA

- Build repeatable lifestyle-business models across locations
