# Realization — Client Services & Pricing

## Service 1: Single Property Purchase Consultation

### What's Included
- Consultation and business plan building
- Personal review and monthly consultation hours
- Identifying assets and document translation management
- NIF issuance and local bank account opening
- Assistance with bank financing
- Representation in negotiations and offer submission
- Contract review and managing professional staff
- Closing details
- **Bonus:** Free personalized property tour

### Pricing (Based on Property Purchase Price + VAT)

| Property Price Range | Fee Structure |
|---------------------|---------------|
| Up to €160,000 | Fixed fee: €6,765 |
| €160,001 - €250,000 | 4.5% + VAT |
| €250,001 - €500,000 | 4% + VAT |
| €500,001 - €750,000 | 3.5% + VAT |
| Above €750,001 | Custom pricing |

- **Typical duration:** 3-6 months per purchase
- **Additional months:** €369/month (€300 + VAT)
- **Payment options:** Monthly or 3-Stage (Downpayment, upon CPCV, upon DEED)

### Special Clauses
- **Finder's fee:** If client buys a property presented by Realization without using services
- **25% reduction:** If client finds the property independently
- **NIF cancellation:** €250 per person if terminated after NIF issuance but before purchase

## General Contract Terms

### Key Principles
- Services for various businesses/real estate
- Maximum flexibility for client to realize profit
- Final investment strategy is **solely the client's decision**
- Expected income scenarios are **not concrete assurances**
- All materials are proprietary (written consent required)

### Cancellation Terms
- Engagement terminates at end date of each milestone
- No refund for completed stages
- Breach: written warning → if unrepaired after CPCV, 50% of remaining balance
- Client withdrawal for private reasons: paid amounts not refunded
- Force Majeure: agreement on partiality

### Required Client Documents
- Specific documents required (list available upon request)
- Official documents in Portuguese/English; other languages need official translation + apostille

### Tax Responsibility
- Taxable income from investment is client's exclusive responsibility
