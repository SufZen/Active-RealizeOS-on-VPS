# BOA Architecture — Project Phases

> Entity: ENT-05 | Standard phases for architectural projects under Arena umbrella.

---

## Phase Overview

Every architectural project follows 4 phases. Arena Habitat is the primary project.

```
Phase 1: Planning & Permitting
    ↓
Phase 2: Licensing & Detailed Design
    ↓
Phase 3: Construction Administration
    ↓
Phase 4: Delivery & Close-Out
```

---

## Phase 1: Planning & Permitting

**Activities:**
- Concept design development
- Site analysis and constraints mapping
- Regulatory research (municipal planning rules, building codes)
- PIP (Pedido de Informacao Previa) preparation and submission
- Initial engineering coordination (structural, MEP)
- Preliminary cost estimation input to budget

**Deliverables:**
- Concept design drawings
- Site analysis report
- PIP submission package
- Preliminary specifications

**Key coordination:**
- Asaf (Project Director): strategic direction, budget alignment
- SFRG (Arc. Ramiro Guinote): design execution
- Camara Municipal: regulatory engagement

---

## Phase 2: Licensing & Detailed Design

**Activities:**
- Detailed architectural design development
- Building permit preparation and submission
- Specialty engineering integration (structural, MEP, thermal, acoustic, fire safety)
- Material and finish specification
- Construction documentation (drawings, schedules, details)
- Cost review with Finance Controller

**Deliverables:**
- Full architectural drawings (plans, sections, elevations, details)
- Building permit submission package
- Engineering coordination documents
- Material specification schedules
- Construction details and sections

**Key coordination:**
- SFRG: design development and documentation
- Engineering specialists: integrated design
- Camara Municipal: permit review and approval
- Roy (Finance Controller): cost alignment with budget

---

## Phase 3: Construction Administration

**Activities:**
- Contractor coordination and shop drawing review
- Regular site visits and quality inspections
- Design conflict resolution
- Change order management (design implications)
- Fiscalizacao coordination (independent supervision)
- Progress documentation

**Deliverables:**
- Shop drawing review comments
- Site visit reports
- Design change documentation
- As-built markup tracking

**Key coordination:**
- General Contractor: construction execution
- Fiscalizacao: independent quality verification
- Asaf: technical strategy decisions
- Roy: budget impact of any changes

---

## Phase 4: Delivery & Close-Out

**Activities:**
- Final inspection coordination
- Defect identification (snag list)
- As-built documentation preparation
- Form 4 support (Utilizacao license)
- Unit handover support
- Project archive compilation

**Deliverables:**
- Final inspection reports
- Snag list and resolution tracking
- As-built drawings
- Project close-out documentation

**Key coordination:**
- Camara Municipal: Form 4 inspection and approval
- General Contractor: defect resolution
- Meirav (Capital & Sales): unit handover coordination
