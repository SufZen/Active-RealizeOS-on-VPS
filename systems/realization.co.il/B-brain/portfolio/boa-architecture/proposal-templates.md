# BOA Architecture — Proposal Templates

> Entity: ENT-05 | Standard proposal format for architectural projects.

---

## Standard Proposal Format

All BOA architecture proposals follow this structure. Validity: 14 days from issue date.

```markdown
# Architecture Proposal

**Project:** [Project Name]
**Client:** [Client Name / Entity]
**Date:** [YYYY-MM-DD]
**Proposal Reference:** [BOA-YYYY-###]
**Valid Until:** [Date + 14 days]

---

## 1. Project Understanding

[2-3 paragraphs summarizing what the client needs and what the project involves.
Include: location, type, scale, any constraints discussed.]

## 2. Scope of Services

### Phase 1: Planning & Permitting
- [List of specific services]
- [Deliverables]

### Phase 2: Licensing & Detailed Design
- [List of specific services]
- [Deliverables]

### Phase 3: Construction Administration
- [List of specific services]
- [Deliverables]

### Phase 4: Delivery & Close-Out
- [List of specific services]
- [Deliverables]

## 3. Exclusions

[What is NOT included in this proposal. Be specific.]
Common exclusions:
- Structural engineering (separate specialist)
- MEP engineering (separate specialist)
- Interior design beyond basic specification
- Landscape design
- Permits and licensing fees (client cost)

## 4. Timeline

| Phase | Duration | Milestones |
|-------|----------|-----------|
| Phase 1 | [X weeks/months] | [Key milestone] |
| Phase 2 | [X weeks/months] | [Key milestone] |
| Phase 3 | [Duration of construction] | [Key milestone] |
| Phase 4 | [X weeks] | [Key milestone] |

## 5. Fee

| Phase | Fee |
|-------|-----|
| Phase 1 | [Amount] |
| Phase 2 | [Amount] |
| Phase 3 | [Amount] |
| Phase 4 | [Amount] |
| **Total** | **[Total Amount]** |

All fees exclude VAT (23%).

## 6. Payment Terms

[Payment schedule tied to phases or milestones]
Typical: 30% at engagement, 30% at Phase 2 start, 30% at Phase 3 start, 10% at completion.

## 7. Terms & Conditions

- Revisions: [X] rounds of revisions included per phase. Additional revisions at [hourly rate].
- Intellectual property: Drawings become client property upon full payment.
- Termination: Either party may terminate with 30 days written notice. Fees for completed work are non-refundable.
- Liability: Limited to the fee amount for this project.

## 8. Acceptance

This proposal is valid for 14 days from the date above.
To proceed, please sign below and return a copy.

_________________________
[Client Name / Authorized Signatory]
Date: _______________
```
