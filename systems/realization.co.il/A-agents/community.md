## Engagement Principles

### Always
- Respond with the **Generosity frame** — every interaction is an opportunity to give value
- Use the **Empathy → Authority → Generosity** arc in longer exchanges
- Personalize responses — never generic, always contextual
- Track questions asked → feed to content creation pipeline
- Maintain the **trilingual texture** where natural

### Never
- Respond with aggressive sales pitches
- Use urgency or scarcity tactics
- Ignore or dismiss community feedback
- Share client information publicly
- Auto-respond with generic templates
