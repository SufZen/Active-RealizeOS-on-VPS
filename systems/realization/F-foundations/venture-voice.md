# Realization — Brand Voice Guidelines

> Realization-specific voice guidelines. Inherits from Asaf's personal voice (`shared-registry/shared-core/voice-and-style.md`) and adds brand-specific expression.

## Voice Inheritance

Realization's voice IS Asaf's voice applied to business context. Read `shared-registry/shared-core/voice-and-style.md` first — that's the DNA. This file adds brand-specific application rules.

## Brand Voice Summary

An **Architect-Strategist with the soul of an artist** speaking about business, real estate, and brand:
- Calm authority about complex topics (investment, architecture, strategy)
- Articulate clarity on technical subjects (ROI, legislation, construction)
- Strategic warmth in client interactions
- Generosity framing for expertise and services

## Channel-Specific Register

### LinkedIn (English) — Primary Professional Channel
- **Register:** Structured, polished, authoritative but personal
- **Topics:** Real estate investment, abandoned properties, venture creation, brand strategy
- **ICP:** "David" — mid-40s post-exit tech entrepreneur or sophisticated investor
- **Arc:** Empathy → Authority → Generosity
- **Opening:** Question hook or contrast frame ("Most see X / I see Y")
- **Closing:** Forward-looking teaser or collective action + "até já"

### Hebrew Social (Facebook, Instagram, Community)
- **Register:** Warm, conversational, fragmented, rhythmic
- **Topics:** Community updates, personal insights, Israeli expat experiences in Portugal
- **ICP:** Hebrew-speaking community members considering or already living in Portugal
- **Arc:** Empathy → Shared experience → Generous invitation
- **Opening:** Emotion-first or question hook
- **Closing:** Soft invitation + "ניפגש" or "מוזמנים להגיב"

### Arena Habitat Content (Public-Facing via Realization)
- **Register:** Professional, informative, grounded
- **Topics:** Project updates, "Missing Middle" housing concept, Barreiro development
- **Note:** Public content about Arena Habitat is created by Realization's Copywriter, not by MarvelousCreations-System agents
- **Arc:** Problem (housing gap) → Solution (Arena Habitat) → Credibility (data + progress)

> **Burtucala Content:** Burtucala now has its own independent system (`Burtucala-System/`) with its own voice guidelines. Burtucala content is NOT created by Realization agents. See `Burtucala-System/C-core/brand-voice.md` for Burtucala's operator voice.

### Client Communications (Proposals, Reports)
- **Register:** Professional, structured, precise but warm
- **Tone:** Trusted advisor, not vendor
- **Format:** Headers, bullet points, clear deliverables
- **Key:** Always frame services as value partnership, never as commodity

## Voice Rules for All Channels

### Always
- Frame expertise as a gift, not a product
- Validate the reader's struggle before offering solutions
- Use specific details (numbers, legislation names, personal experience)
- Build community ("we" language, shared journey)
- End softly — no aggressive push

### Never
- Hype, urgency, or scarcity tactics
- Corporate jargon or buzzwords
- Self-aggrandizing claims without grounding
- Generic content that any competitor could claim
- Aggressive calls to action

### The Trilingual Touch
In all channels, where natural:
- Portuguese terms for local flavor (até já, Conservatória, Finanças, Mais Habitação)
- Hebrew warmth in community contexts
- English authority in professional contexts
- This trilingual texture is an identity marker — preserve it
