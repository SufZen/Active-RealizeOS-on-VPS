# Realization — Service Standards

> Quality standards, disclaimers, and client principles for all Realization services.

## Client Principles

### How We Serve
- **Trusted partner, not vendor** — we are in the relationship for the long term
- **Maximum flexibility** for the client to realize profit
- **Transparent about complexity** — never oversimplify to close a deal
- **Quality over speed** — we don't rush if it compromises the result
- **Personal involvement** — Asaf is personally involved in every stage

### What We Promise
- Strategic clarity in every engagement
- Aesthetic quality in every deliverable
- Honest assessment of opportunities and risks
- Structured, step-by-step process with clear milestones
- Regular communication and reporting

### What We Don't Promise
- Guaranteed financial returns — investment strategy is solely the client's decision
- Expected income scenarios are based on market data, not concrete assurances
- Market volatility may affect budgets (up to 15% deviation considered normal)
- We consult and guide — final decisions always rest with the client

## Quality Standards

### Content
- Every piece of published content goes through Gatekeeper review
- Voice DNA compliance is mandatory, not optional
- Anti-patterns trigger automatic revision
- Specific over vague: numbers, details, real examples required

### Client Deliverables
- All proposals follow standard templates (BOA Architecture format)
- Financial projections include clear disclaimers
- Contract clauses use standardized templates from shared-library
- All materials are proprietary — require written consent for private/commercial use

### Real Estate
- Investment analysis follows the ROI Modeling Protocol (MTH-17)
- Property assessment follows BOA Anomaly Diagnosis Protocol (MTH-16)
- Construction budgets consider Portugal market labor costs (€500-1300/sqm)
- Budget analysis aligned with architectural recommendations
- Renovation recommendations typically Mid/Max level for maximized ROI

### Operations
- Dedicated bank accounts for specific ventures where possible
- Payments approved only after verified reports
- Financial reports submitted quarterly
- Accounting managed by certified accountant (Audax / Mario)
