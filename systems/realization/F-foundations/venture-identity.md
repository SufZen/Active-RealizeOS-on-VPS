## What We Do

Realization Portugal is the operational real estate arm of the Realization ecosystem. We provide three core services in the Portuguese market: property sales mediation, real estate project development, and on-site project management.

## Why We Exist: Potential Arbitrage in Physical Space

We see properties sitting below their Highest and Best Use — and we close that gap. Vacant lots, abandoned buildings, underperforming assets in Portugal's urban fabric are inefficiency gaps waiting to be resolved. Our job is to identify that locked potential and bring it to full realization through professional mediation, development, and management.

## Three Operational Services

### 1. Real Estate Mediation
- Licensed property sales mediation (AMI CEMediação 25459)
- Multi-channel property marketing (Idealista, Kyero, JamesEdition, LuxuryEstate, realization.pt)
- Buyer qualification, lead management, and sales pipeline operations
- Trilingual service delivery (Portuguese, English, Hebrew)
- Portfolio management across price segments (entry, mid-market, luxury)

### 2. Real Estate Project Development
- Acting as GP (General Partner) or promoter on development projects
- Investment structuring and capital coordination
- Licensing and permitting navigation (Camara Municipal, Turismo de Portugal)
- Partnership coordination with architects, engineers, and contractors

### 3. On-Site Project Management
- Construction and renovation supervision on behalf of owners/investors
- Budget tracking, milestone management, and contractor coordination
- Quality control and compliance monitoring
- Progress reporting and stakeholder communication

## Urban Infill & ESG Philosophy

We prioritize projects that heal the urban environment. Our preferred targets are "urban scars" — vazios urbanos (vacant urban spaces) and predios devolutos (abandoned buildings) within existing urban fabric. We believe in:
- Reusing existing infrastructure rather than consuming new land
- Healing wounds in the streetscape — empty lots, derelict buildings, underperforming properties
- Creating profitable assets that are also sustainable and positively impactful
- Adding value to communities through thoughtful rehabilitation

This is a strong directional preference across all our projects and territories, not an absolute constraint.
