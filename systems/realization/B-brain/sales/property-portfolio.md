### Critical Findings from Audit

1. **Photo deficit is the #1 problem across the portfolio.** Average: 12.4 photos. Required minimum: 15-20 (entry) / 25-30 (luxury). Every listing except Combatentes (18) is critically under-photographed.

2. **Zero floor plans across all 8 audited listings.** Idealista penalizes missing floor plans. This is a portfolio-wide fix.

3. **No PT descriptions on any listing.** All were English-only. In Portugal, PT must be the primary language. Optimized PT+EN descriptions are now ready for all 8.

4. **No AMI disclaimer on any listing.** Compliance gap — all optimized descriptions now include AMI 25459.

5. **Area inconsistencies on 3 listings** (Ligeiro: 50 vs 47m², Combatentes: 133 vs 120m², Baía: 169 vs 172m²). Must be verified and corrected before applying new descriptions.

6. **Floor level inconsistencies on 2 Porto listings** (Ground floor vs Floor -1). Must be verified.

7. **Porto studios have zero investment data** despite being positioned as investment products. AL revenue data is the single biggest conversion lever missing.

8. **3 listings stale (>1 month without update)** — Ligeiro, Combatentes, Arroios. Simple refresh (edit + save) resets the algorithm.
