### Common Issues Found Across Portfolio

1. **No Portuguese descriptions** — all 8 listings were English-only
2. **No AMI 25459 disclaimer** — compliance gap on all 8
3. **No floor plans** — missing on all 8
4. **Insufficient photos** — average 12.4 vs minimum 15-20
5. **Generic filler language** — "Don't miss out", "ticks all the boxes", "exceptional opportunity"
6. **No investment data** — particularly critical for Porto AL studios
7. **Area inconsistencies** — 3 listings had mismatches between description and structured fields
8. **Stale listings** — 3 of 8 not updated for >1 month
