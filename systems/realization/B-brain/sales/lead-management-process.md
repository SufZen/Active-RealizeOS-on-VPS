> End-to-end lead handling from first contact to deed signing.

## 1. Lead Scoring Framework

Every incoming lead receives a score based on the following criteria. Score immediately upon first contact or qualification call.

### Scoring Criteria

| Criterion | Points | Description |
|---|---|---|
| **Called (phone contact)** | +3 | Lead initiated a phone call or answered our call. Highest intent signal. |
| **Budget confirmed** | +3 | Lead has stated a specific budget range that matches our inventory. |
| **Investor profile** | +2 | Lead is purchasing for investment (rental yield, capital appreciation, development). |
| **Timeline < 3 months** | +2 | Lead intends to purchase within 3 months. Active buyer. |
| **International buyer** | +1 | Non-Portuguese buyer. May have specific needs (visa, tax) but often higher-value transactions. |
| **Burtucala referral** | +3 | Lead referred through the Burtucala network. Pre-qualified, high trust. |
| **Repeat client** | +2 | Has purchased through us before. Known quantity. |
| **Pre-approved financing** | +2 | Has bank pre-approval or proof of funds. |
| **Specific property inquiry** | +1 | Asked about a specific listing (vs. general inquiry). |
| **Agent/broker referral** | +1 | Referred by another licensed agent (AMI co-broke). |

### Maximum Possible Score: 20 points

## 5. Follow-Up Cadence

### Standard Follow-Up Sequence

| Day | Action | Channel | Content |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Day 0** | First response | Email + WhatsApp | Template response (see above). Acknowledge inquiry. Offer dossier. |
| **Day 1** | Follow-up if no reply | WhatsApp | Short message: "Bom dia [Nome], enviei-lhe informação ontem sobre o [imóvel]. Posso ajudar com alguma questão?" |
| **Day 3** | Value-add follow-up | Email | Send a relevant comparable sale or market insight for the area. Position as helpful, not pushy. |
| **Day 7** | Phone call attempt | Phone | First call attempt. If no answer, leave voicemail and send WhatsApp: "Tentei contactá-lo/a. Estou disponível para conversar quando for conveniente." |
| **Day 14** | Alternative property suggestion | Email | "Entretanto, temos um novo imóvel que poderá interessar-lhe..." Send a similar property or a market update. |
| **Day 30** | Re-qualification | Email or phone | "Olá [Nome], passaram algumas semanas desde o nosso último contacto. Continua à procura de imóvel na zona de [X]?" |
| **Day 60** | Nurture transfer | Email | If still no engagement, move to monthly newsletter list. Remove from active pipeline. |

### Tier A (Hot Lead) — Accelerated Cadence

| Day | Action |
|---|---|
| Day 0 | Immediate call + WhatsApp + email with full dossier |
| Day 0 (evening) | If no showing scheduled, follow up by WhatsApp |
| Day 1 | Second call attempt if no connection |
| Day 2 | Send video walkthrough or virtual tour link |
| Day 3 | Personal message from Asaf if not yet connected |
| Day 5 | Offer flexible showing time including weekend |
| Day 7 | Escalation review — why hasn't this converted? |

## 6. Pipeline Stages

```
NEW → CONTACTED → QUALIFIED → SHOWING SCHEDULED → SHOWING DONE → OFFER → NEGOTIATION → CPCV → DEED
```

### Stage Definitions

| Stage | Definition | Entry Criteria | Exit Criteria |
|---|---|---|---|
| **New** | Lead received, not yet contacted | Inquiry received from any channel | First response sent |
| **Contacted** | First response sent, awaiting reply | Response sent | Lead replies or qualification call completed |
| **Qualified** | Lead scored and tier assigned | Scoring criteria assessed | Score assigned, budget/timeline confirmed |
| **Showing Scheduled** | Viewing date confirmed | Mutual availability agreed | Showing date in calendar |
| **Showing Done** | Property viewed | Showing completed | Feedback collected from buyer and owner |
| **Offer** | Buyer has submitted an offer | Written or verbal offer received | Offer communicated to owner |
| **Negotiation** | Price/terms under discussion | Counter-offer or discussion initiated | Agreement on terms or deal collapses |
| **CPCV** | Promissory contract signed | Terms agreed, lawyer review complete | CPCV signed, deposit paid (typically 10-20%) |
| **Deed** | Final deed (escritura) completed | CPCV conditions met, financing approved | Deed signed at notary, keys delivered |

### Average Stage Durations (Portuguese Market)

| Stage Transition | Entry Segment | Mid Segment | Luxury Segment |
|---|---|---|---|
| New → Qualified | 1-3 days | 2-5 days | 3-7 days |
| Qualified → Showing | 3-7 days | 5-10 days | 7-14 days |
| Showing → Offer | 1-7 days | 7-21 days | 14-60 days |
| Offer → CPCV | 7-14 days | 14-30 days | 21-45 days |
| CPCV → Deed | 30-60 days | 45-90 days | 60-120 days |
| **Total avg.** | **~2-3 months** | **~3-5 months** | **~4-8 months** |

## 7. Segment-Specific Handling

### Entry Market (<EUR 250K)

- **Approach**: Fast-close, volume-oriented
- **Lead profile**: Local investors, first-time buyers, small portfolio builders
- **Key data points to present**: Gross yield, comparable rents, area appreciation rate, condominium costs
- **Showing strategy**: Group showings acceptable. Saturday morning slots for efficiency.
- **Decision timeline**: Push for decision within 7 days of showing
- **Collateral**: One-page investment summary with key numbers
- **Common objections**: "I found something cheaper" → Show data on why location/condition justifies premium. "What about renovation costs?" → Provide renovation cost estimates if applicable.

### Mid Market (EUR 250K - EUR 450K)

- **Approach**: Moderate timeline, consultative
- **Lead profile**: Upgraders, expats, medium investors, building developers
- **Key data points to present**: Development feasibility (buildings), comparable transactions, neighborhood trajectory, rental market data
- **Showing strategy**: Individual showings. Provide printed dossier at showing.
- **Decision timeline**: Allow 2-3 weeks. Follow up with market intelligence.
- **Collateral**: Full property dossier with floor plan, caderneta predial, comparable sales, neighborhood analysis
- **Common objections**: "The renovation costs are uncertain" → Provide contractor estimates. "Other agents have similar buildings" → Differentiate on project status, location quality, and our market knowledge.

### Luxury (EUR 450K+)

- **Approach**: Personal touch, white-glove service
- **Lead profile**: HNW individuals, international relocators, lifestyle buyers, Golden Visa seekers
- **Key data points to present**: Architectural provenance, neighborhood prestige, capital preservation data, lifestyle narrative
- **Showing strategy**: Private showings ONLY. Asaf present for score 8+ leads. Offer coffee/wine at showing.
- **Decision timeline**: Patience. Luxury decisions take 1-3 months. Maintain relationship without pressure.
- **Collateral**: Premium dossier (printed on quality paper or branded PDF) with professional photography, architect information, neighborhood cultural guide
- **Common objections**: "I'm still comparing options" → Respect timeline, send periodic lifestyle content. "The price seems high" → Present comparable transactions and argue scarcity. "I need my lawyer/advisor to review" → Facilitate direct communication with legal team.

## 10. Lead Management Rules

### Non-Negotiable Rules

1. **No lead goes unanswered for more than 2 hours during business hours.** Period.
2. **Every lead gets scored within 24 hours of first contact.**
3. **Every showing gets a same-day follow-up call** to both the buyer and the owner.
4. **Every offer gets communicated to the owner within 1 hour** of receipt.
5. **Pipeline is updated in real-time.** If a showing happened, the stage moves immediately.
6. **No lead is deleted.** Cold leads move to nurture, never to trash.
7. **Language preference is recorded at first contact.** All subsequent communication in that language.
8. **Source is recorded at first contact.** No "unknown" sources after 7 days.

### Weekly Pipeline Review (Every Monday)

- Review all leads by stage
- Identify stalled leads (no movement in 7+ days)
- Review conversion rates by stage
- Identify Tier A leads requiring Asaf attention
- Plan showing schedule for the week
- Review and update lead scores based on new information
