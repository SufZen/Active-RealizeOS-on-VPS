## 2. Portal-Specific Requirements and Best Practices

### 2.1 Idealista (All Segments)

**Profile**: Portugal's dominant property portal. 80%+ of buyer search traffic.

| Requirement | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Language** | Portuguese (primary listing), English (additional) |
| **Photos** | Min 15, max 50. No watermarks. Landscape orientation. |
| **Description length** | 800-1,500 characters optimal. Longer = better quality score. |
| **Structured fields** | Complete ALL fields. Missing fields = quality score penalty + invisible to filtered searches. |
| **Floor plan** | Required for 85%+ quality score |
| **Energy certificate** | Required (Portuguese legal obligation) |
| **Refresh cadence** | Monthly description review. Photo reorder quarterly. |
| **Paid products** | Destaque (EUR 30-80/mo), Super Destaque (EUR 80-150/mo) |
| **Response time** | Track via Idealista's internal metrics — agencies with slow responses get penalized |

**Best practices:**
- Publish new listings Tuesday-Wednesday morning (highest traffic)
- Never use hashtags (Idealista penalizes them)
- Complete the "Extra" fields: orientation, views, air conditioning, heating — these are search filters
- Respond to ALL contacts within Idealista's platform (not just by phone) to maintain response rate metrics

### 2.2 Kyero (Mid + Luxury)

**Profile**: International property portal. Strong in UK, Nordic, and Northern European markets.

| Requirement | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Language** | English (primary), Portuguese |
| **Photos** | Min 10, ideally 20+ |
| **Description** | English-optimized. Shorter sentences. Include practical information for international buyers (visa, taxes, purchase process). |
| **Pricing** | EUR only. Include "price on request" option for ultra-luxury. |
| **XML feed** | Can be automated via CRM/portal management tools |

**Best practices:**
- Emphasize lifestyle and relocation angle (not just investment)
- Include information about the purchase process for foreigners
- Mention proximity to international schools, airports, English-speaking services
- Kyero buyers are typically at earlier stages — expect longer decision cycles

### 2.3 Green-Acres (Luxury)

**Profile**: French and Northern European focused portal. Strong in lifestyle properties.

| Requirement | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Language** | French (primary), English |
| **Photos** | High quality, lifestyle-oriented |
| **Description** | Lifestyle narrative. Emphasize climate, food, culture, quality of life. |
| **Property types** | Villas, premium apartments, rural estates perform best |

**Best practices:**
- French buyers are drawn to Portugal for lifestyle — lead with that narrative
- Emphasize proximity to the coast, climate comparisons with France
- Include area photos (not just property) showing lifestyle context
- NHR/tax regime information is highly relevant for French audience

### 2.4 JamesEdition (Luxury + Special)

**Profile**: Global luxury marketplace. Buyers are HNW individuals seeking premium properties.

| Requirement | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Language** | English |
| **Photos** | Professional architectural photography. Minimum 15. |
| **Description** | Premium narrative. No investment jargon. Lifestyle and exclusivity only. |
| **Minimum price** | Generally EUR 500K+ to be relevant on the platform |
| **Presentation** | Must feel "magazine quality" — amateur listings damage brand |

**Best practices:**
- Only list properties with professional photography
- Descriptions should read like editorial content, not classifieds
- Highlight unique features: architect-designed, historical provenance, panoramic views
- Include neighborhood as a lifestyle proposition
- This is a "browsing" platform — buyers discover, they don't necessarily search with strict criteria

### 2.5 LuxuryEstate (Luxury + Special)

**Profile**: International luxury portal with strong Italian and Southern European presence.

| Requirement | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Language** | English, Portuguese |
| **Photos** | Professional quality required |
| **Description** | Luxury-focused narrative |
| **Strengths** | Good visibility in Southern European luxury market |

**Best practices:**
- Similar approach to JamesEdition but slightly broader audience
- Effective for EUR 450K-1M range that may be below JamesEdition's typical price point
- Good complementary channel to JamesEdition

### 2.6 realization.pt (All Segments)

**Profile**: Our own website. Full control over presentation and messaging.

| Requirement | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Language** | PT, EN, HE |
| **Photos** | Full gallery, highest resolution |
| **Description** | Most complete version — no platform character limits |
| **Additional content** | Floor plans, downloadable dossiers, area guides, virtual tours |
| **SEO** | Optimize for long-tail keywords: "[neighborhood] apartment for sale", "[city] investment property" |

**Best practices:**
- Every property should have its own dedicated page
- Include a "Download Property Dossier" CTA for lead capture
- Blog/content marketing: neighborhood guides, market updates, investment analysis
- Track visitor behavior to understand which properties generate most interest
- This is our controlled environment — use it to showcase depth of service

### 2.7 Facebook Groups (Entry)

**Profile**: Community-based property discovery. High volume, lower quality leads.

| Requirement | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Format** | Short post with 4-6 photos and key details |
| **Groups** | "Imóveis em Lisboa", "Apartamentos Porto", "Investimento Imobiliário Portugal", expat groups |
| **Frequency** | 1-2 posts per week per property. Do not spam. |
| **Tone** | Casual, informative, community-appropriate |

**Best practices:**
- Focus on entry-segment properties only (group audiences are price-sensitive)
- Lead with price and location in the first line
- Use Facebook's native photo carousel feature
- Respond to comments publicly to show engagement
- Follow group rules strictly — getting banned costs access to large audiences
- Include WhatsApp number for direct contact (Facebook Messenger conversion is lower)

## 3. Burtucala Cross-Promotion Protocol

### Scope: BUILDINGS ONLY

**Burtucala cross-promotion applies ONLY to buildings suitable for asset-based ventures (development projects, multi-unit rehabilitation). NEVER apartments.**

### Protocol

| Element | Realization Portugal | Burtucala |
|---|---|---|
| **Voice** | Professional real estate mediation | Investment and venture development |
| **Domain** | realization.pt | burtucala.com |
| **Listing** | Full property listing with AMI | Investment opportunity framing |
| **Audience** | Buyer seeking to purchase | Investor/developer seeking venture partner |
| **Legal entity** | Realization Portugal Lda. | Burtucala entity |
| **AMI** | Realization AMI number | N/A (not a mediation activity) |

### Separation Rules

1. **Separate domains**: Never cross-link in a way that confuses which entity is the mediator
2. **Separate voices**: Realization speaks as the licensed real estate mediator. Burtucala speaks as the venture/investment platform.
3. **Clear disclaimers**: Any Burtucala content referencing a Realization-listed building must state: "Mediação imobiliária por Realization Portugal, AMI [número]"
4. **Referral tracking**: Track leads that originate from Burtucala with source code "BUR" in the CRM
5. **Commission handling**: If a buyer comes through Burtucala's network for a Realization-listed building, standard Realization commission applies. Burtucala referral fee (if any) is a separate arrangement between entities.

### Eligible Properties

From current portfolio, the following qualify for Burtucala cross-promotion:
- **Building R. António (Setúbal)** — EUR 379K, 6 fractions, approved project

### Non-Eligible (Examples)

- Any apartment (T0, T1, T2, etc.) — regardless of price
- Single-family homes (moradias)
- Properties without development/venture potential

## 4. Israeli Channel Strategy

### Overview

Israel represents a significant buyer pool for Portuguese real estate, driven by:
- Investment diversification outside Israel
- Golden Visa / residency pathway (where applicable)
- Cultural affinity (Sephardic heritage, growing PT-IL business ties)
- Favorable EUR/ILS dynamics
- Asaf's personal network and Hebrew-language capability

### Channel Tactics

| Channel | Action | Frequency |
|---|---|---|
| **Hebrew brochures** | Prepare for every mid + luxury property | Per listing |
| **Israeli RE Facebook groups** | Post translated listings with investment data | 2-3x/month |
| **Israeli RE Telegram groups** | Share new listings and market updates | Weekly |
| **Monthly Hebrew newsletter** | Market update + featured properties + investment analysis | Monthly |
| **Asaf's personal network** | Direct outreach for matching opportunities | Ongoing |
| **Israeli RE events/expos** | Attend or present at Israeli investor events | 2-3x/year |
| **WhatsApp broadcast list** | Curated list of qualified Israeli contacts | Bi-weekly |

### Hebrew Newsletter Structure

```
1. שוק הנדל"ן בפורטוגל — עדכון חודשי
   (Monthly market update — key trends, data points)

2. נכסים מובחרים
   (Featured properties — 2-3 properties with photos and key data)

3. זווית השקעה
   (Investment angle — rental yields, capital appreciation, tax benefits)

4. תהליך הרכישה
   (Purchase process tip — one aspect of buying in Portugal, demystified)

5. צרו קשר
   (Contact — Hebrew-speaking contact details)
```

### Israeli Buyer Concerns (Address Proactively)

| Concern | How We Address It |
|---|---|
| "Is Portugal safe for investment?" | Present stability data, EU membership, rule of law index |
| "Tax implications in Israel?" | Refer to qualified cross-border tax advisor (do not advise directly) |
| "Can I manage remotely?" | Present property management options, AL management services |
| "Golden Visa eligibility?" | Current rules and eligible investment types |
| "How does the purchase process work?" | Step-by-step guide in Hebrew |
| "What are the ongoing costs?" | IMI, condominium, insurance, management fee breakdown |

## 5. Luxury-Specific Channels

### JamesEdition Strategy

- Only list properties EUR 500K+ with professional photography
- Budget: listing fee varies (typically EUR 100-300/month per property)
- Update listings monthly with fresh photos or angles
- Track referral traffic to measure ROI

### LuxuryEstate Strategy

- Good for EUR 450K-1M range
- Broader audience than JamesEdition
- Lower cost per listing
- Complement with JamesEdition for full luxury coverage

### Sotheby's International Realty Referral

- For properties EUR 1M+, explore referral arrangement with Sotheby's Portugal
- Sotheby's has unmatched brand recognition in ultra-luxury
- Referral fee: typically 25-35% of commission
- Currently relevant for: Arroios (EUR 1.45M), potentially Douradores post-renovation (EUR 950K)
- This is a referral arrangement, not co-listing — we remain the listing agent

### Architecture and Design Media

**For design-notable properties only (currently: Arroios, potentially Miguel Pais)**

| Platform | Type | Audience | Cost |
|---|---|---|---|
| **ArchDaily** | Listing / editorial | Architects, designers, design-conscious buyers | Free listing, paid promotion EUR 200-500 |
| **Dezeen** | Editorial / showcase | Global design community | Paid editorial EUR 500-2,000 |
| **Wallpaper*** | Editorial / listing | Luxury lifestyle, HNW design-conscious | Paid feature EUR 1,000+ |
| **Monocle** | City guide / listing | Global cosmopolitan audience | Relationship-based |

**Approach:**
- Frame the property as a story, not a listing
- Lead with architecture and design narrative
- Include architect/designer credits
- Professional architectural photography is mandatory (not real estate photography)

### Instagram / Pinterest

**For visually compelling properties only.**

| Platform | Content Strategy | Frequency |
|---|---|---|
| **Instagram** | Carousel posts (8-10 images), Reels (15-30s walkthrough), Stories (behind-the-scenes of showings/renovation) | 2-3 posts/week |
| **Pinterest** | Pin each property photo individually with keyword-rich descriptions. Create boards by style/location. | 10-15 pins per property |

**Instagram best practices:**
- Use the @realization.pt account (or create if needed)
- Hashtags: #LisbonRealEstate #PortugalProperty #LuxuryLisbon #ChiadoApartment (location-specific)
- Geotag every post with the neighborhood
- Stories for showing invitations, market updates, behind-the-scenes
- Reels for property walkthroughs (trending format, highest reach)
- Never post unfinished or low-quality content — every post represents the brand

### LinkedIn (Luxury)

- Publish articles about the Lisbon/Porto luxury market
- Share individual property stories as posts (not listings — tell a story)
- Target: international executives, expats, investors
- Asaf's personal profile and Realization company page
- Effective for reaching decision-makers who don't browse property portals

### Channel ROI Decision Rules

- **Cost/contact > EUR 50**: Review listing quality on that channel before cutting spend
- **Cost/contact > EUR 100**: Consider removing property from that channel
