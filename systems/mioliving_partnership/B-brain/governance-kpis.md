# MioLiving Partnership — Governance & KPIs

## Governance Framework

### Weekly Commitment

Every partner commits to a minimum of **10 hours per week** dedicated to partnership activities. Attendance at regular partner forums is mandatory. These forums are the primary coordination mechanism — deal reviews, financial updates, open items, and cross-arm alignment.

---

### Domain Authority

Each partner has autonomous decision-making authority within their own domain. Professional decisions that fall clearly within a domain do not require group approval:

| Partner | Domain Authority |
|---|---|
| Asaf | All systems, technology, CRM, automations, and venture building delivery |
| Shirly | All Italy-side operations, supplier selection, contractor management, regulatory navigation |
| Rafi | All hospitality strategy, feasibility modeling, and hotel deal origination |
| Merav | All client service protocols, sales process, investor community, and design (with Mika) |

Cross-domain decisions — particularly those affecting shared resources, brand, or financial commitments — require group discussion.

---

### Financial Decision Limits

| Decision | Authority |
|---|---|
| Up to €200 | Any individual partner may act independently |
| Above €200 | Requires CFO approval before commitment |

The CFO role and approval process are defined within the shared system managed by Asaf. All financial actions are logged and visible to all partners.

---

### Transparency Standard

All financial actions, deal statuses, and pipeline movements are managed in Asaf's shared system. No deals enter the system as "closed" until formally logged. The shared system is the single source of truth for the partnership.

---

## Year 1 KPIs

| Business Arm | Target | Cadence |
|---|---|---|
| Retail Investor Escort | 1 closed deal | Per month |
| Residential Development (Flip) | 1 closed deal or signed LOI | Per quarter |
| Hospitality (Deal Making) | 1 closed deal or signed LOI | Per quarter |
| Design & Interior Design | Minimum 2 proposals submitted | Per month |
| Venture Building | 2 strategic clients acquired | Per year |

KPIs are reviewed at quarterly forums. Shortfalls trigger a review of sourcing and pipeline health before adjusting targets.
