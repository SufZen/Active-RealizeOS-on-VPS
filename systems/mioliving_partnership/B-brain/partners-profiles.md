# MioLiving Partnership — Partner Profiles

## Overview

The partnership is built on four complementary domains. Each partner is the authority in their domain, contributes a distinct IP stack, and retains that IP individually on exit.

---

## Rafi — Hospitality Domain

**Domain:** Hospitality strategy, feasibility, and deal origination

**Key contributions:**
- Leads Arm 3 (Hospitality) — both the active Deal Making track and the future Hotel Development track
- Builds and owns relationships with hotel management companies and institutional hospitality investors
- Develops the financial feasibility models used to qualify and present hotel opportunities to investors
- Brings deep expertise in heritage asset development and repositioning strategies for the Italian market

**IP ownership on exit:**
- Advanced economic models for hospitality feasibility analysis
- Network with hotel management companies and institutional investors
- Heritage asset development strategies (methodology and frameworks)

---

## Shirly — Italy (Local Operations & Network)

**Domain:** Italian on-the-ground operations, supplier network, and regulatory navigation

**Key contributions:**
- Primary local presence in Italy — the operational backbone for all cross-border execution
- Manages the Italian contractor and supplier trust network built through direct relationships
- Navigates Italian regulatory processes, bureaucratic pathways, and licensing requirements on behalf of clients and the partnership
- Provides access to local authority databases and shortcuts that compress timelines and reduce risk
- Partnership account holder for initial capital

**IP ownership on exit:**
- Italian supplier and contractor "trust network" (contacts, relationships, vetting criteria)
- Regulatory shortcuts and bureaucracy hacking — proprietary process knowledge
- Local authority databases and licensing process documentation
- Regulatory knowledge for the European real estate market

---

## Asaf — Strategy & Systems

**Domain:** Business strategy, AI/technology systems, and venture building methodology

**Key contributions:**
- Leads Arm 5 (Venture Building) — strategy and system architecture for foreign entrepreneurs
- Brings the Realization methodology and AI-driven operations experience to MioLiving's infrastructure
- Designs and manages the shared financial and operational systems (CRM, automations, dashboards)
- Architects the partnership's "profit center" structure and overall business design
- Responsible for financial transparency — maintains the shared system all partners work in

**IP ownership on exit:**
- Venture Building methodologies (frameworks, playbooks, delivery processes)
- "Profit center" structural architecture for real estate businesses
- CRM automations and API connection architectures
- Financial management dashboards and reporting systems
- Architectural planning methods for the European market

---

## Merav — Service & Deal-Making

**Domain:** Client experience, investor relations, and sales conversion

**Key contributions:**
- Leads the 360° service methodology that defines client experience across Arm 1 (Investor Escort)
- Builds and manages the investor community — the distribution channel that feeds deals across all arms
- Co-leads Arm 4 (Design & Interior Design) with Mika
- Owns the sales conversion process — from first contact through signed agreement
- Manages and grows the Investor Persona model unique to MioLiving's client profile

**IP ownership on exit:**
- "360° Service" methodology and client experience protocols
- Sales scripts and conversion frameworks
- Investor community structure and community building methodology
- Investor Persona model (MioLiving-specific client profiling system)
