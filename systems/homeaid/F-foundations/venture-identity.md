# HomeAid — Venture Identity

## Business Name & Tagline
Name: HomeAid
Tagline: Essential property services, done right.

## Mission
Provide reliable, transparent property services supporting the full lifecycle of property value creation and management in the Lisbon and Setúbal regions.

## Ecosystem Role: Supporting the Physical Dimension

HomeAid supports the Realization ecosystem's physical dimension of Potential Arbitrage. When the ecosystem creates or rehabilitates assets, HomeAid maintains and protects that realized value — ensuring properties stay at their Highest and Best Use through reliable, quality services.

## Core Services (15 total)
Cleaning, Maintenance, Gardening, Pool Maintenance, Technical Assistance, Electricity, Plumbing, Painting, Carpentry, Locksmith, Security, Property Management, Air Conditioning, and more.

## Pricing Model
**70/20/10** (calculated after direct material costs):
- 70% → Executing Team Member(s)
- 20% → HomeAid Overhead & Profit
- 10% → Lead/Client Source

## Service Area
Lisbon and Setúbal regions, Portugal.

## Team Size
5 members.

## Core Values
1. Reliability — show up on time, every time
2. Transparency — clear pricing, no hidden costs
3. Quality — workmanship warranty on every job (30/60/90-day per task type)

## Brand Personality
Professional, Reliable, Approachable, Efficient
