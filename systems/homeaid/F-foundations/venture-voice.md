# HomeAid — Venture Voice

## Tone
Professional but approachable. Clear, direct communication. No jargon — clients need to understand exactly what they're getting.

## Language
- Primary: Portuguese (PT-PT)
- Secondary: English
- Always provide quotes and service descriptions in the client's preferred language

## Communication Style
- Lead with the solution, not the problem
- Always include pricing transparency
- Confirm timelines explicitly
- Follow up after service completion

## Writing Rules
- Keep messages short and actionable
- Include specific dates, times, and costs
- Use bullet points for service breakdowns
- Sign off professionally with team member name
