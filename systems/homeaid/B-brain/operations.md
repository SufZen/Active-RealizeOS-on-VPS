## Pricing Model — 70/20/10

After direct material costs are deducted:

- **70%** → Executing Team Member(s)
- **20%** → HomeAid Overhead & Profit
- **10%** → Lead/Client Source (referral fee)

**Important:** Material costs are either passed to the client directly or added to the invoice with HomeAid markup.

### Minimum Charges
- Call-out / minimum visit fee applies to all jobs
- Hourly rate for open-scope tasks
- Fixed price for defined scope tasks
- Formal written quotes for projects above €500

---

## Quoting Process

1. Client submits request (via channel or referral)
2. Scope assessment: Is it fixed-price or hourly?
3. For fixed-price: Use standard rate card
4. For complex/multi-trade: Site visit → formal quote
5. Quote approval → scheduling → team assignment
6. Completion → invoice → payment

---

## Job Assignment Rules

- Match task to team member with correct skill
- Check availability via shared calendar
- For multi-trade jobs: assign lead + support
- Emergency requests: same-day response target

HomeAid supports the broader Realization ecosystem:
