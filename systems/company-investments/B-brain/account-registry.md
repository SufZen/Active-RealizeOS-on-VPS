# Account Registry

## Purpose

Tracks company treasury accounts, investment sleeves, and manually maintained private allocations.

## Source Types

- Operating cash and reserve accounts
- Broker exports
- Treasury spreadsheet snapshots
- Manual records for private deals and real estate interests

## Rules

- Separate operating liquidity from investable capital.
- Every source entry must include last updated date.
- If a source is stale, the system must say so before recommending action.
