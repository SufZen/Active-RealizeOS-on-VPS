# Company Treasury And Investment Policy

## Mandate

Protect company liquidity while allowing controlled return-seeking allocations only inside approved sleeves.

## Core Principles

- operating liquidity comes first
- reserve capital remains separate
- investable sleeves need explicit rules and review cadence
- all proposals must be source-backed and approval-aware

## Required For Every Proposal

- policy rule invoked
- source data used
- confidence level
- invalidation conditions
- approval requirement
