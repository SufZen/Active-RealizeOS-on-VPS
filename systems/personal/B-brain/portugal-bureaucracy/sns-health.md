# SNS Healthcare

## What This Is

The SNS (Serviço Nacional de Saúde) is Portugal's National Health Service — universal, publicly funded, and accessible to all residents. As a Portuguese citizen and Setúbal resident, you have full access to primary care through the local health centre (centro de saúde), specialist referrals, emergency services, and prescription medications via the public system.

The SNS is structured in layers:
- **Primary care**: Centro de Saúde (your GP — médico de família)
- **Secondary care**: Hospital (specialists — médico especialista)
- **Emergency**: INEM/urgência

## When You Need This

- Booking a routine or urgent appointment at the health centre
- Registering or changing your assigned GP (médico de família)
- Getting a specialist referral (consulta de especialidade)
- Renewing chronic medication prescriptions without an appointment
- Accessing lab results or medical records online
- After an emergency, coordinating follow-up care
- If a team member at HomeAid needs guidance on accessing SNS (as their employer, you may be asked)

## Required Documents

- Cartão de Cidadão or SNS user number (número de utente)
- NIF (often requested at reception)
- If registering at a new health centre after moving: proof of address (comprovativo de morada) — electricity/water bill or bank statement with Setúbal address

## Step-by-Step Process

### Finding Your Centro de Saúde in Setúbal

Setúbal has two main primary care units (UCSP/USF) within the ACES Setúbal e Palmela cluster:
- **Centro de Saúde de Setúbal** — Rua António Maria Eça, 2900 Setúbal
- **USF units** (Unidades de Saúde Familiar) — check sns.gov.pt for the unit covering your exact address

To confirm which unit covers your address:
1. Go to sns.gov.pt
2. Search "Encontre o seu Centro de Saúde" or use the SNS app
3. Enter your postal code to see the assigned facility

### Booking an Appointment

**Online (preferred):**
1. Go to sns.gov.pt and log in (with NIF + password or Chave Móvel Digital)
2. Navigate: Marcação de Consultas > Cuidados de Saúde Primários
3. Select your health centre and doctor
4. Choose available slot

**By Phone:**
- SNS 24 line: **808 24 24 24** (24/7, free call from Portuguese landlines)
- SNS 24 can: triage symptoms, book appointments, issue prescriptions for chronic conditions
- Also available: Centro de Saúde de Setúbal direct reception (call in the morning, lines often busy)

**In Person:**
- Arrive at the health centre and request an appointment at reception
- Morning walk-in slots (consulta de recurso) are available for urgent same-day issues

### Assigning or Changing Your GP (Médico de Família)

If you are registered in Setúbal but have not been assigned a GP, or want to change:
1. Login to sns.gov.pt
2. Navigate: Cuidados de Saúde Primários > Inscrição / Alteração de Médico
3. OR call SNS 24 (808 24 24 24) and ask about médico de família assignment
4. OR visit the Centro de Saúde reception with your Cartão de Cidadão and proof of address
5. If there is no GP available (a common situation in Portugal), you will be placed on a waiting list but can still access the health centre for urgent consultations

### Getting a Specialist Referral (Consulta de Especialidade)

1. See your GP first — they assess and issue a referral (pedido de consulta de especialidade) if clinically justified
2. The referral goes into the SIGIC system (Sistema de Gestão de Inscritos para Cirurgia / Consultas)
3. You will receive an SMS or letter with an appointment at Hospital de Setúbal (Centro Hospitalar de Setúbal) or an alternative facility
4. If waiting time exceeds guaranteed timelines, you may be entitled to treatment at a private provider at SNS cost (voucher SIGIC) — ask your GP
5. Track your referral status: sns.gov.pt > Consultas Externas

### Prescription Renewal (Receita Eletrónica)

Chronic medication prescriptions can often be renewed without a face-to-face appointment:
1. Contact SNS 24 (808 24 24 24) — they can issue renewals for stable chronic conditions
2. Use the SNS app (downloadable on iOS/Android): medication history and prescription requests
3. The prescription is issued electronically (receita electrónica) — collect medication at any pharmacy with your Cartão de Cidadão
4. Pharmacies have access to the central prescription database — no paper needed

### Emergency Services

- **INEM (medical emergency)**: **112** (European emergency number)
- INEM dispatches ambulances and paramedics
- For non-life-threatening urgent issues: use SNS 24 (808 24 24 24) first — they triage and direct you to the appropriate service
- **Hospital de Setúbal (Centro Hospitalar de Setúbal)**: Rua Camilo Castelo Branco, 2910-446 Setúbal | +351 265 549 000
- Urgência (A&E) is 24/7 — bring Cartão de Cidadão; expect triagem (triage) process (Manchester system)

### SNS App

Download "MySNS" or "SNS 24" app:
- View your medical record summary
- Book and cancel appointments
- View prescriptions
- Access lab results
- Check vaccination history

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

- **Going to A&E for non-emergencies** — wait times are long (4-8 hours); SNS 24 will direct you to a faster route for non-urgent issues
- **Not checking your número de utente (SNS user number)** — you need this for appointments; find it on your Cartão de Cidadão or via sns.gov.pt
- **Not registering a change of address** — if you move within Setúbal or from another municipality, update your address at the health centre or via sns.gov.pt to maintain your GP assignment
- **Assuming prescriptions are automatically renewed** — chronic meds may expire; check expiry dates and request renewal via SNS 24 before running out
- **Missing specialist appointments** — if you miss a SIGIC appointment without cancelling, you lose your slot and the waiting period restarts; cancel in advance via sns.gov.pt if unable to attend

## Estimated Timeline & Cost

| Service | Timeline | Cost |
|---|---|---|
| GP appointment (routine) | 2-10 days via booking | Free (SNS) or small taxa moderadora |
| GP appointment (urgent, walk-in) | Same day | Free or €5-7 taxa moderadora |
| Specialist consultation (public) | 1-6 months (SIGIC wait) | Free (SNS) |
| Emergency/Urgência | Immediate triage | Free (SNS); some taxa moderadora applies |
| SNS app / SNS 24 | Immediate | Free |
| Private consultation (alternative) | 1-7 days | €60-150 (out of pocket) |

Note: Taxa moderadora (co-payment) is waived for certain conditions and exemptions — if you have a long-term illness, check if you qualify for exemption at your health centre.
