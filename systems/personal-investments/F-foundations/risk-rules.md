# Risk Rules

## Hard Rules

- No recommendation may exceed the active authority mode.
- Concentration and liquidity concerns must be surfaced before upside scenarios.
- Stale or missing source data downgrades confidence and may block action.
- Manual assets cannot be auto-priced without an explicit source note.

## Veto Conditions

- policy contradiction
- missing holdings reconciliation
- unresolved stale data
- position sizing that violates current guardrails
