# Investment Policy

## Mandate

Preserve and compound personal capital through a policy-driven, multi-asset portfolio.

## Default Position

- Primary mode: long-term allocation discipline
- Tactical activity: optional and size-limited
- Cash is an allowed decision when opportunity quality is weak

## Required For Every Proposal

- policy rule invoked
- source data used
- confidence level
- invalidation conditions
- approval requirement

## Review Cadence

- Daily brief
- Monthly review
- Quarterly policy review
