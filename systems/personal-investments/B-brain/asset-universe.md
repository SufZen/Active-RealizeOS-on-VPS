# Asset Universe

## Included In v1

- Public equities
- ETFs and index exposure
- Bonds and cash equivalents
- Crypto allocations when explicitly tracked
- Private deals and real estate as manual-input sleeves

## Rules

- Private and illiquid assets are tracked for portfolio context, not auto-priced by the system.
- Tactical ideas must remain subordinate to the long-term policy.
- New asset classes require an explicit policy update before they become routine.
