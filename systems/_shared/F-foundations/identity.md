# Identity — WHO Asaf Is

> This file defines the PERSON — universal across all entities. Not brand-specific. For the full ecosystem manifesto, see `manifesto.md`.

## Core Mandate

**"Building realities through Potential Arbitrage."** We identify locked potential — in spaces, systems, and people — and bring it from dormancy into full realization. This is not just a business model; it is a way of seeing the world.

This plays out across three dimensions:
- **Physical:** Transforming vacant land and abandoned buildings into productive, sustainable assets
- **Systemic:** Replacing outdated bureaucracy with AI, automation, and data-driven intelligence
- **Human:** Every person operates in their Zone of Genius — never wasting potential on mismatched tasks

## The Intersection

Life and work are not separate domains — they intersect intentionally. Asaf brings the full self to every project and decision. The way he designs a space is the way he designs a business is the way he designs a life.

## Multi-Local Philosophy

Living across borders — both literally and conceptually:
- **Portugal** — home base (Setúbal)
- **Spain** — ground for expansion (Barcelona and beyond)
- **Israel** — origin, family, first community

The aim: build a life that functions across multiple centers, each being meaningful, connected, and real. Designing lifestyle "from the inside out" and assisting others in exploring and building their own.

## The Approach

An **Architect-Strategist with the soul of an artist** — the "Pipe Builder" who designs systems from zero to one:
- Radiates calm authority in the middle of chaos
- Speaks with articulate clarity
- Appreciates the curvature of a wall as much as the logic of a business model
- Connects different fields into one coherent vision
- Builds the machine, then hands it off for execution — never gets stuck in operational treadmill work

## How He Thinks

- **Macro to micro:** Starts with the big vision, then structures every detail to serve it
- **Inside-out design:** Never starts with the surface — always with the essence
- **Potential Arbitrage lens:** Always asking — what is the gap between current state and highest-and-best-use?
- **Bridge-builder:** Between strategy and aesthetics, between Israel and Portugal, between vision and execution
- **Systems thinker:** Prefers step-by-step, clear segmentation, and collaborative iteration
- **Balance seeker:** Between absorbing and creating, between structure and spontaneity

## Inspirations

- **Google & Samsung** — pinnacle of tech, service, and aesthetics
- **Leonardo da Vinci & Rabi Moshe Ben Maimon** — simplifying the complex, connecting different fields
- **Philosophic architects** — those who define the essence of "space"

## The Mission Beyond Business

A deep desire to impact many — not from ego, but from a mission. A deeper calling to create things that outlast, that serve, that inspire. Building ventures that are profitable AND deeply meaningful — for their owners, users, and communities. Prioritizing projects that heal the urban environment and add sustainable value to the world.
