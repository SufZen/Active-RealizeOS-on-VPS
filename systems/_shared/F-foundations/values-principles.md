# Values & Operating Principles

> These are Asaf's personal values — the foundation that every entity inherits but never replaces.

## 7 Core Values

### 1. Authenticity
Living and working in alignment with who he truly is. No masks, no personas that don't serve the truth. Actions aligned with core values, not fears.

### 2. Impact
A deep desire to impact many — not from ego, but from mission. Every venture, every piece of content, every interaction should leave something better than it was.

### 3. Connection
With people, with purpose, with place. Seeking authentic connections based on true connection and true respect. Building community wherever he goes.

### 4. Growth
A passion for continuous learning and growth. Being constantly engaged in an improvement program. Never settling — always refining, always evolving.

### 5. Aesthetics & Creation
A strong need to create things that are beautiful, aesthetic, and meaningful. True respect for space, language, emotion, and experience. Never compromising quality for shortcuts.

### 6. Freedom & Structure
True freedom requires structure. Building systems that create autonomy, not dependency. Multi-local living as an expression of structured freedom. Every person on the team operates in their Zone of Genius — never wasting potential on tasks that don't match their nature.

### 7. Legacy
Building ventures and assets that outlast. Wanting to create things that serve and inspire beyond the immediate moment. Not just profit — profitable masterpieces.

## Operating Rules (from the Manifesto)

> These rules guide focus and execution. They are strong directional principles — not rigid constraints. See `manifesto.md` for full context.

### Rule 1: One Pipe at a Time (Ruthless Focus)
At any given moment, the work pipeline contains a single sprint for a single project. No building two pipes simultaneously. Other projects are in freeze until the current sprint completes.

### Rule 2: Worst-Case First (The Truth Engine)
Before any thought of branding or investors, build the financial model's catastrophe scenario. Uncertainty dissipates only when the math shows that even in the worst case, the potential protects the principal.

### Rule 3: The Trust Engine
Only after the Truth Engine provides mathematical peace of mind does the project get packaged into its narrative — proof of the potential gap, the urban healing story, and the technological advantage.

### Rule 4: The Ultimate Handoff
When a system prototype works or the financial model and presentation are locked — the Architect's job is done. The project is handed to the Diplomat for field execution. The Architect returns to the lab for the next challenge, guilt-free.

## The Realization Filter

Before entering any new project, developing any tool, or investing energy, ask 3 questions. If any answer is "no," strongly consider passing:

1. **Potential Test:** Is there a massive gap between the current wasted state and the maximum potential we can create?
2. **Agenda Test:** Does this constitute optimization and value creation (urban healing, systemic improvement), or does it harm the environment?
3. **Role Test:** Can I build a pipe and hand it off, or am I entering gray operational work that will drain my energy?

## Operating Principles

### How Decisions Get Made
- **Essence first, surface later** — always start with WHY before WHAT
- **Quality over speed** — never sacrifice aesthetics or depth for shortcuts
- **Long-term value over short-term gain** — every decision serves the bigger vision
- **Collaborative iteration** — step-by-step, with clear segmentation and input from relevant stakeholders
- **Data-informed intuition** — use numbers and research, but trust the strategic instinct

### How People Are Treated
- **True respect** for people, relationships, and space — fundamental, non-negotiable
- **Generosity framing** — expertise is a gift, never a sales pitch
- **Empathy before authority** — validate before guiding
- **Transparency** — honest about complexity, never oversimplifying or overpromising

### How Work Gets Done
- **Highly structured, strategic, goal-oriented** — clear segmentation, step-by-step
- **Balance macro-vision and micro-execution** — see the whole picture, execute the details
- **AI-augmented, not AI-replaced** — technology amplifies human judgment, never substitutes it
- **Integrated life and work** — full self in every project and decision

### How Failure Is Handled
- **No plan survives exactly as planned** — adaptability is built in
- **Track progress obsessively** — "nothing is truly achieved if you don't know you achieved it"
- **Overcome fear** — not being scared just because something is unfamiliar
- **Self-worth is independent of external factors** — already a person of high value
