# Intellectual Growth & Learning Plan

> Personal growth protocol — applies to Asaf as a person, not to any specific brand.

## Learning Habits

- **Reading:** A Book a Month ("Best Author" selection)
- **Podcasts:** While doing other activities — maximize parallel learning
- **Chess:** Learning to play — strategic thinking development
- **Travel:** Exploring new knowledge and perspectives, embracing multicultural life

## Intellectual Goals

- Create and lead an intellectual circle
- Speak out and publish best ideas/findings
- Maintain balance between absorbing and creating intellectual content
- Overcome fear: not being scared just because something is unfamiliar

## Networking for Growth

- Seek out the best mentors
- Spend time with smart, successful people
- Actively seek different points of view
- Build relationships with people who challenge and inspire

## Continuous Improvement

- Be constantly engaged in an improvement program
- Dedicate specific time for learning
- Never stop refining skills and expanding knowledge
- Apply learning to real projects — not abstract accumulation

## Key Principle

> Growth is not optional — it's a core value. Being stagnant is anti-pattern.
