# Well-Being Protocol — Health, Fitness, Spiritual, Planning

> Personal protocol — applies to Asaf as a person, not to any specific brand.

## Health & Fitness

### Nutrition
- Focus on high-quality food
- Never be cheap on good food
- Avoid impulsive buying (no shopping when hungry or without a list)

### Exercise
- **Super Fun Sports:** Tennis, Padel, Matkot, Football, Beach/Water sports
- **Gym:** Classes + Personal Training Plan
- **Sensual Dancing:** Salsa, Bachata
- **Principle:** Avoid wasting time on useless or unenjoyable exercise — exercise should be fun

### Self-Care
- Massage: aim for at least once a month
- Express discomfort if not enjoying it
- Never be cheap on self-care

---

## Spiritual Practices

- **Meditation:** At least once a day — protect this time from scheduling disturbances
- **Spontaneity:** Embrace it alongside structured planning
- **Moral conduct:** Uphold strong moral and ethical standards
- **Authenticity:** Living in alignment with core values, not fears
- **Mission-driven:** Impact stems from a deeper calling beyond ego

---

## Planning Protocol

### Core Belief
Being in control of one's life and time. Plans need adaptability — "no plan survives exactly as planned."

### Daily Planning
- 15 minutes every morning with tea
- Maintain the highest priority for what is truly important

### Weekly Planning
- Relaxed weekly process: Saturday or Sunday afternoon, alone
- Categorized into **13 life areas** to ensure attention to all aspects
- Review the **Life Vision Synopsis** to plan the upcoming week
- Set priorities based on vision and quarterly goals
- Clearly define weekly commitments and act accordingly

### Tracking
- "Nothing is truly achieved if you don't know you achieved it"
- This can only be done with oversight and measurement
- Track progress consistently across all areas

## Core Beliefs (Inner Game)

- Self-worth is independent of external factors
- Already a person of high value
- Consciously control truths and beliefs to serve desires
- Understanding motivations and desires is important
- Being realistic about possibilities for self-realization
- Focus is on gratitude: "Your life is where your focus and attention are"
