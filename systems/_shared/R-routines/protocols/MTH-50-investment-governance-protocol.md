# MTH-50 — Investment Governance Protocol

## Purpose

Defines the minimum governance bar for internal investment operations across personal and company systems.

## Rules

- Paper mode is the default launch mode.
- Every recommendation must state its approval requirement.
- Risk review is mandatory for any rebalance or trade proposal.
- Authority upgrades require an explicit logged decision.
- Shared dashboards may compare systems, but ordinary workflows must keep contexts separate.
