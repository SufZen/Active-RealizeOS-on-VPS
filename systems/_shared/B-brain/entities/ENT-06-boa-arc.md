# Boa Arc — Architectural Design & Vision

An architectural brand providing design and vision services for investment-oriented projects. Operates under Marvelous Creations LDA (ENT-05).

## Core Focus

- Architectural design for investment-oriented renovation and construction projects
- Design vision that balances aesthetic excellence with investment ROI
- Project planning through delivery (Pre-Design → Interior Design → Specifications → Detailed Design → Licensing → Execution)

## Project Phases & Deliverables

### Planning Stage (Milestone 1-2 / L1-L4)
- Pre-Design (schematic layout, simple 3D, review)
- Interior Design (moodboard, schematic proposal)
- Specifications (material sets, choosing options)
- Detailed Design (plans, coordination)
- Shopping List & Purchase

### Licensing Process (Milestone L3-L7)
- Architecture Project preparation (Plans, Sections, Elevations, Details)
- Written Pieces (Descriptive Memory, Terms of Responsibility, Schedule, Estimate)
- Structural Survey & Report
- Specialties Project & Coordination
- Submission → Feedback → Approval → Construction Execution Documents

### Execution Deliverables
Floor Plans, Alterations, Elevations, Sections, Details, Landscape, Facade, Mechanical Plans (Electrical, Plumbing, HVAC), Specifications, Schedules, Interior Design Plans.

## Proposal Structure

Standard BOA proposals include: Header, Agreement Parties, Project ID, Budget Guidelines, Scope by Stages, Deliverables, Fees, Payment Milestones, Bank Details, General Remarks, Legal & Compliance, Signatures. Validity: 14 business days.

## Payment Milestone Structures

Vary by project type: Standard Renovation, Planning & Licensing, Phased Projects, Interior Design. Structured as percentages of total fee at key stages.

## Integration

BOA Architecture provides the design intelligence for Realization's Space & Real Estate pillar and Marvelous Creations' construction project management.
