# HomeAid — Property Services

Essential services supporting the functionality and value of properties, complementing Realization's investment and management activities. Ensures the full lifecycle of property value creation and management.

## Key Services (15 total)

Cleaning, Maintenance, Gardening, Pool Maintenance, Technical Assistance, Electricity, Plumbing, Painting, Carpentry, Locksmith, Security, Property Management, Air Conditioning, and more.

## Pricing Model

**70/20/10** (calculated after direct material costs):
- 70% → Executing Team Member(s)
- 20% → HomeAid Overhead & Profit
- 10% → Lead/Client Source

Minimum charge / call-out fee applies. Transparency with team about the structure.

## Service Area

Lisbon and Setúbal regions.

## Team Size

5 members.

## Operational Details

- Scheduling via simple shared calendar
- Skill matching for job assignment
- Quoting: fixed prices, hourly rates, formal quotes
- Material costs: client pays direct or HomeAid purchases/adds to invoice
- Workmanship warranty: 30/60/90-day per task type
