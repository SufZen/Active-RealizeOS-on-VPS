# Second Base — A New Model for Living Internationally

A groundbreaking concept defining the future of lifestyle for individuals choosing to live between multiple cities and countries.

## Core Vision

Enable and facilitate intentional, multi-local living systems — providing tools and frameworks for a globally connected yet deeply rooted lifestyle.

## Target Audience

Individuals who consciously choose a multi-local lifestyle, valuing connection to place and community in multiple locations.

## Model

Hybrid integrating content, automation, personal story, and community building.

## Realization's Role

Realization (ENT-01) is the engine behind Second Base, providing:
- Business design
- System architecture
- Content strategy
- Creative direction

## Philosophy

Embodies Realization's commitment to "building realities" and "designing experiences." Embodies Asaf's personal multi-local lifestyle (Portugal, Spain, Israel) as a proof of concept.
