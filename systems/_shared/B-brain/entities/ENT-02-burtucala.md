# Burtucala — Venture-Building & Real Estate Consulting

A venture studio that designs and launches space-based businesses in Portugal. Burtucala orchestrates the full path from site and permits to operations, brand, and launch.

## Positioning

"Where Real Estate Meets Business."

Core belief: A venture succeeds when property decisions (site, licensing, capex, layout) and business decisions (offer, staffing, pricing, yield, SOPs) are made together, inside one joined-up plan.
