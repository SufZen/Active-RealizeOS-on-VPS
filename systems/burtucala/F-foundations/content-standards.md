# Burtucala — Content Standards

> Entity: ENT-02 | Quality rules for all Burtucala content.

---

## Golden Rules

1. **No invented details.** Every claim must come from real (anonymized) experience or verifiable data.
2. **No em dashes.** Use periods, commas, or colons. This is non-negotiable.
3. **Numbers before adjectives.** Quantify before you describe.
4. **One pillar, one format per piece.** Each content piece maps to exactly one of the 4 pillars and one of the 6 formats.
5. **Constraints before options.** Show the limitation, then the pathway.
6. **Plain English.** If a Portuguese term is used, translate and explain it immediately.

## Fact-Checking Protocol

Before any content is published:
- [ ] All numbers verified against source (government data, direct experience, or cited report)
- [ ] Portuguese legal/licensing claims checked against current regulations
- [ ] No client names or identifiable details unless explicitly approved
- [ ] Pricing reflects current market (check if data is older than 6 months)
- [ ] All Portuguese terms translated and contextualized

## Content Workflow

```
Content Creator drafts
    ↓
Gatekeeper reviews (checklist below)
    ↓
Revision if needed
    ↓
Final approval
    ↓
Publish + log in M-memory
```

## Gatekeeper Checklist

### Voice Check
- [ ] No em dashes anywhere in the text
- [ ] English only (no untranslated Portuguese without explanation)
- [ ] Operator tone (not academic, not sales-heavy, not influencer)
- [ ] Numbers appear before descriptive claims

### Structure Check
- [ ] Mapped to one content pillar (P1/P2/P3/P4)
- [ ] Follows one content format structure (Reality Log/Bureaucracy Journal/etc.)
- [ ] Appropriate length for platform (Blog: 800-1500, LinkedIn: 150-600)
- [ ] CTA present and appropriate

### Accuracy Check
- [ ] No invented case studies or fake examples
- [ ] Data sources recent (within 6 months) or marked as historical
- [ ] Legal/licensing information current
- [ ] Financial figures include relevant caveats

### Framing Check
- [ ] Constraints shown before options
- [ ] No hype language (revolutionary, game-changing, unprecedented, amazing)
- [ ] Honest about challenges without being dramatic
- [ ] Specific next steps or actionable takeaway included

## Compliance

- Investment disclaimers required on any content discussing specific returns or opportunities
- No guarantees of returns or outcomes
- Privacy: no client data without explicit written consent
- All content respects GDPR and Portuguese data protection law (Law No. 67/98)
- Website footer disclaimers apply to all published content
