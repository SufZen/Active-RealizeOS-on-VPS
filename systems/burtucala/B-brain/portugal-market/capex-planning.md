# Burtucala — Capex Planning Reference

> Entity: ENT-02 | Renovation costs, MEP, FF&E, and soft costs by venture type.

---

## Important Note

Construction costs fluctuate with material prices, labor availability, and location. All figures below are ranges based on field experience and market data. Always verify current costs with contractors before advising clients. **Last updated: 2026-Q1. Flag any data older than 6 months.**

**Reference benchmark:** Portugal's official average construction cost for IMI purposes in 2026 is **€570/sqm** (Ordinance No. 471/2025/1). Actual renovation costs for commercial spaces are typically 2-4x this figure depending on scope.

---

## Cost Categories

Every renovation budget should include these categories:

| Category | Description | Typical % of Total |
|----------|-------------|-------------------|
| **Hard Costs** | Construction, structural, MEP (mechanical/electrical/plumbing) | 55-65% |
| **FF&E** | Furniture, fixtures, and equipment | 15-25% |
| **Soft Costs** | Architecture, engineering, permits, legal, project management | 10-15% |
| **Contingency** | Unexpected costs (ALWAYS include) | 10-15% |

## Common Cost Drivers (Often Underestimated)

1. **Kitchen extraction systems** — €15K-40K for a full restaurant extraction system. Often requires building permit.
2. **Elevator installation** — €30K-60K. Required for accessibility in multi-floor hospitality.
3. **Seismic reinforcement** — €100-300/sqm. Common in pre-1960 buildings.
4. **Asbestos removal** — €50-150/sqm. Mandatory in many older buildings.
5. **Fire safety systems** — €10K-30K. Sprinklers, alarms, emergency lighting, signage.
6. **Accessibility compliance** — €5K-20K. Ramps, accessible bathrooms, doorway widths.
7. **Outdoor terrace build-out** — €300-600/sqm. If F&B terrace is part of the concept.

## Soft Cost Budget Guide

| Item | Range | Notes |
|------|-------|-------|
| Architecture + engineering | 8-12% of construction cost | Higher for complex heritage buildings |
| Licensing and permits | €3K-15K | Depends on venture type and municipality |
| Legal fees (property + business) | €3K-8K | Includes contract review, entity setup |
| Project management | 5-8% of total | Or fixed fee for smaller projects |
| Insurance (construction phase) | 0.5-1% of construction cost | Required for contractor agreements |

## Budget Planning Rules

1. **Always include contingency.** 10% minimum. 15% for heritage buildings or first-time developers.
2. **Separate hard costs from soft costs.** Clients often forget soft costs add 15-25% on top.
3. **FF&E is not optional.** Budget it separately and early. Delivery times can be 8-16 weeks.
4. **Cash flow matters.** Map payment milestones to construction phases. Most contractors require 30-50% upfront.
5. **Get 3 quotes minimum.** Portuguese construction pricing varies widely between contractors.
6. **Factor in timeline costs.** Every month of delay has a carrying cost (rent, loan interest, opportunity cost).
