# Burtucala — Marketing Strategy

> Entity: ENT-02 | Core marketing framework. Source: Marketing Strategy Doc (March 2026).

---

## Strategic Intent

Burtucala positions itself as an operator-expert building a visible, content-heavy authority brand that reduces uncertainty for foreign founders. Rather than amplifying excitement, the strategy acknowledges that Portugal's bureaucracy is slow and complex, then demonstrates how to design smart execution within that reality.

---

## Primary Conversion Path

Two main calls-to-action across all content:

1. **Book a Fit Call** — Initial qualification filter. 15-20 minutes. Mutual assessment.
2. **Request a Blueprint** — Paid diagnostic that monetizes strategy work. Delivers sequenced execution plans, financial ranges, licensing highlights, and partner introductions with a 30-day Q&A window.

The Blueprint is the critical entry product. It converts interest into revenue and demonstrates Burtucala's methodology before a larger engagement.

**CTA Assignment Guide:**
- Use "Book a Fit Call" for awareness-stage content (energy management, general tips, community-building)
- Use "Request a Blueprint" for consideration-stage content (deal analysis, licensing confusion, space design problems, investment decisions)

---

## Target Audience

The ideal client is a foreign founder-operator or aligned investor who:
- Researches extensively before committing
- Seeks "honest view of reality without drama"
- Values "clear thinking about risk, timelines, energy, returns"
- Prefers concrete examples over aspirational narratives
- Uses LinkedIn regularly for professional insights
- Conducts detailed Google searches on regulatory and operational specifics

---

## Channel Strategy

### LinkedIn
- Position as operator-expert sharing grounded stories and specific insights
- Engage meaningfully in comments without selling
- Target data-driven and experience-based content about Portugal-specific operations
- Hook in first line. One insight. One CTA. No hashtag spam (3 max).

### Blog / Website (burtucala.com)
- Create **entity-based clusters** mapping real institutions and concepts foreigners struggle with
- Key entities to cluster around: Finanças, Alojamento Local vs. Empreendimento Turístico, licensing by municipality, Camara Municipal, Segurança Social, RNAL, HACCP
- Structure content for **Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)**: clean sections, definitions, internal linking
- Each article should define Portuguese terms, link to related articles, and be structured for AI readability

### Facebook Groups
- Join relevant expat and entrepreneur communities
- Contribute helpful knowledge first without promotional messaging
- Offer checklists and frameworks; maintain confidentiality and reliability
- Never post links to Burtucala content unprompted. Share value, build reputation.

### Newsletter
- Bridge between social proof and paid diagnostics
- 2-4 short sections combining Reality Logs, tips, and dictionary terms
- Link back to full website articles
- Nurture existing contacts and Syndicate prospects

### Partnerships
- Develop referral relationships with lawyers, architects, financing consultants, and relocation specialists
- Position Burtucala as the execution layer that ensures integrated planning across legal, design, and operational concerns

## GEO / Entity-Based Content Strategy

Blog content should be organized into **entity clusters**. Each cluster maps to real Portuguese institutions, processes, or concepts that foreign founders search for.

### How to Structure a GEO-Optimized Article

1. **Define the entity clearly** in the first section (e.g., "Alojamento Local (AL) is Portugal's short-term rental license...")
2. **Use clean H2/H3 headers** that match search queries
3. **Include a Portuguese term glossary** when multiple terms appear
4. **Link internally** to other articles in the same cluster
5. **Provide specific numbers** (timelines, costs, requirements) with dates
6. **End with next steps** (CTA + link to related content)

### Priority Entity Clusters

| Cluster | Key Entities | Content Need |
|---------|-------------|-------------|
| Licensing & Permits | AL, ET, RNAL, Camara Municipal, Turismo de Portugal | High — most-searched by new investors |
| Construction & Renovation | Empreiteiro, RJUE, Fiscalização, IMI | Medium — cost and timeline clarity |
| Business Setup | Finanças, Segurança Social, NIF, Atividade | Medium — operational basics |
| Investment Analysis | RevPAR, gross yield, capex/sqm, break-even | High — decision-stage content |
| Space & Operations | HACCP, space programming, F&B licensing | Medium — operator-specific |

## Trust Signals Framework

### What Builds Trust
- Transparent case studies with real timelines and challenges
- Specific operational knowledge (not generic advice)
- Honest acknowledgment of delays and complexity
- Under-promising and over-delivering
- Consistent, reliable communication

### What Erodes Trust
- Overpromising or oversimplifying
- Vague corporate jargon without substance
- Influencer-style self-promotion
- Evasion or lack of transparency
- Inconsistent messaging

**Rule:** Every content piece should demonstrate at least one trust-building signal. The content brief template includes a trust signal checklist.

## Realization Property Cross-Promotion (Restricted)

Cross-promotion with Realization Portugal RE is **LIMITED to properties matching Burtucala's exact ICP: buildings suitable for asset-based ventures only** (NOT apartments).

Burtucala and Realization are **separate businesses with separate partners and separate storytelling.**

### Rules
- ONLY promote buildings/multi-unit properties that match venture profiles (e.g., Setúbal building €379K)
- Never mix voices: Burtucala voice for Burtucala content, Realization for property copy
- No burtucala.com/properties page — keep domains completely separate
- No generic property cross-promotion of any kind

### When a Matching Building is Available
- Burtucala newsletter: "Property Spotlight" featuring the building with venture-lens analysis
- LinkedIn: investment-logic posts can reference the building as a case study
- Always frame through Burtucala's operator-expert lens, never as a sales listing
