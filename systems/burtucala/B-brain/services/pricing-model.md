## Refund Policy

### Sprint 0 (Diagnostic)
If the client is not satisfied with the Sprint 0 output, they are not obligated to continue. Sprint 0 fee is non-refundable (work was completed), but it acts as a "trust gate." The client pays for Sprint 0 knowing they can walk away.

### Sprint 1-3
Each sprint is paid upfront. No refund for completed sprints. The client can choose not to proceed to the next sprint at any point.

### DFY Compact
50% at kickoff (non-refundable). 50% at delivery. If scope changes are requested, an amendment is required.

### Advisory
Monthly billing. 30-day cancellation notice. No refund for the current month.

### Syndicate
Membership terms per cohort. Success fee payable only on closed deals.

---

## Pricing Philosophy

1. **Transparent.** Prices are published. No hidden fees.
2. **Progressive.** Clients can start small (Fit Call > Strategy Meeting > Sprint 0) and increase commitment gradually.
3. **Fair exit.** Clients can stop after any sprint without penalty.
4. **Value-aligned.** Sprint pricing reflects the work delivered, not the value captured. Success fees (Syndicate) align incentives.
