# Burtucala — Sprint Framework

> Entity: ENT-02 | Sprint 0-3 methodology. Deliverables per sprint. Artifacts list.

---

## Philosophy

Ventures are built in sprints, not in one big plan. Each sprint produces concrete deliverables. The client can stop after any sprint and still walk away with usable work product.

**Core principle:** Design the venture on paper before it costs money in reality.

---

## Sprint Sequence

```
Sprint 0: Diagnostic (Pilot)     →  Go / No-Go decision
    ↓
Sprint 1: Architecture           →  Venture designed on paper
    ↓
Sprint 2: Execution Planning     →  Executable plan with timeline
    ↓
Sprint 3: Pre-Launch             →  Ready to open
    ↓
Run Support (optional)           →  Post-launch stability
```

---

## Sprint 0: Diagnostic (Pilot)

**Duration:** 2 weeks
**Price:** 2,800
**Purpose:** Validate concept viability before committing to a full build.

### Inputs Required
- Client brief (goals, budget range, concept idea, preferred location/area)
- Property details if available (address, sqm, current condition, asking price)
- Timeline expectations

### Activities
- Site-concept fit analysis
- Licensing pre-check (which licenses are needed, estimated timelines)
- Capex order-of-magnitude estimate
- Revenue potential assessment (market context, comps)
- Risk screening

### Deliverables
| # | Deliverable | Format |
|---|-------------|--------|
| 1 | Concept Validation Report | 3-5 page document |
| 2 | Site-Concept Fit Assessment | 1-page scoring matrix |
| 3 | Licensing Path Summary | 1-page overview with timeline |
| 4 | Capex Band Estimate | Range table (low/mid/high) |
| 5 | Go/No-Go Recommendation | Written verdict with reasoning |
| 6 | Sprint 1 Scope | 1-page scope document (if Go) |

### Exit Point
Client receives the diagnostic and decides whether to proceed to Sprint 1. Non-refundable, but the client keeps all deliverables.

---

## Sprint 1: Architecture

**Duration:** 4 weeks
**Price:** 2,400
**Purpose:** Design the entire venture on paper. All major decisions are made in this sprint.

### Inputs Required
- Sprint 0 deliverables (or equivalent if client enters at Sprint 1)
- Confirmed property details
- Budget ceiling
- Branding preferences (if any)

### Activities
- Offer design (what the venture sells, to whom, at what price)
- Space program creation (zones, sqm, flow, capacity)
- Revenue model (streams, pricing, capacity utilization, break-even)
- Licensing action plan (applications, sequence, dependencies)
- Capex budget (line items, not ranges)
- Staffing model (roles, hours, costs)
- Brand brief (positioning, target customer, experience promise)

### Deliverables
| # | Deliverable | Format |
|---|-------------|--------|
| 1 | Venture Architecture Document | 8-15 page comprehensive document |
| 2 | Space Program | Zone table + flow diagram + capacity summary |
| 3 | Revenue Model | Spreadsheet with 3 scenarios |
| 4 | Licensing Action Plan | Timeline + checklist |
| 5 | Capex Budget | Line-item spreadsheet |
| 6 | Staffing Model | Roles/hours/cost table |
| 7 | Brand Brief | 2-3 page positioning document |

### Exit Point
Client has a complete venture design. Can be used independently even if they don't continue.

---

## Sprint 2: Execution Planning

**Duration:** 4 weeks
**Price:** 2,400
**Purpose:** Turn architecture into an executable plan with timelines, contractors, and suppliers.

### Inputs Required
- Sprint 1 deliverables
- Confirmed budget allocation
- Contractor access (or Burtucala sources from partner network)

### Activities
- Pre-opening critical path (milestone sequencing)
- Contractor scope of work drafting
- FF&E specification and sourcing
- Supplier shortlisting and quote collection
- Marketing launch plan
- Day-one operational SOPs

### Deliverables
| # | Deliverable | Format |
|---|-------------|--------|
| 1 | Pre-Opening Critical Path | Gantt or milestone timeline |
| 2 | Contractor Scope of Work | Template per trade |
| 3 | FF&E Specification List | Categorized spreadsheet |
| 4 | Supplier Shortlist | Compared quotes table |
| 5 | Marketing Launch Plan | Channel/timeline/budget document |
| 6 | Operational SOPs (Day-One Set) | 1-page per SOP |

### Exit Point
Client has everything needed to execute. Can manage the build independently from here.

---

## Sprint 3: Pre-Launch

**Duration:** 4 weeks
**Price:** 2,400
**Purpose:** Final preparation before doors open.

### Inputs Required
- Sprint 2 deliverables
- Construction/renovation near completion
- Team hired (or hiring in progress)

### Activities
- Systems setup (POS, booking engine, accounting, communication tools)
- Team onboarding plan and training materials
- Soft-launch protocol design
- KPI dashboard creation (what to measure from day one)
- Quality standards documentation
- 90-day post-launch plan

### Deliverables
| # | Deliverable | Format |
|---|-------------|--------|
| 1 | Systems Setup Checklist | Categorized checklist |
| 2 | Team Onboarding Plan | Week-by-week schedule |
| 3 | Soft-Launch Protocol | Step-by-step guide |
| 4 | KPI Dashboard | Spreadsheet or tool setup |
| 5 | Quality Standards Document | 2-3 page reference |
| 6 | 90-Day Post-Launch Plan | Timeline with milestones |

### Exit Point
Venture is ready to open. Client has all systems and documentation.

---

## Run Support (Optional)

**Duration:** 4-week renewable blocks
**Price:** 800 per block
**Purpose:** Post-launch operational support during the critical first months.

### Activities
- Weekly check-in calls (30 min)
- KPI review and analysis
- Problem-solving and troubleshooting
- SOP refinement based on real operations
- Team coaching and feedback
- Quarterly strategic review (if 3+ blocks)

### Exit Point
Client can stop at the end of any 4-week block.

---

## Sprint Rhythm

Each sprint follows the same weekly rhythm:

| Week | Focus |
|------|-------|
| Week 1 | Kickoff + research + data gathering |
| Week 2 | Analysis + initial drafts |
| Week 3 | Review + refinement + client feedback |
| Week 4 | Finalize deliverables + Sprint Review meeting |

**Sprint Review Meeting:** 60-minute session at the end of each sprint. Present deliverables, gather feedback, confirm Go/No-Go for next sprint.

---

## Artifact Naming Convention

All sprint deliverables follow this format:
```
[Sprint]-[Number]-[Deliverable Name].md
Example: S1-03-revenue-model.xlsx
Example: S2-01-critical-path.md
```

Stored in: `C-creations/venture-build-artifacts/sprint-[0-3]/`
