## Shared Entry Path (All Clients)

Every engagement starts the same way, regardless of which service the client ends up in.

### Step 1: Free Fit Call
- **Duration:** 15-20 minutes
- **Format:** Video call (Zoom/Google Meet)
- **Purpose:** Mutual assessment. Is there a fit between the client's needs and Burtucala's capabilities?
- **Outcome:** Go/No-Go. If Go, schedule Strategy Meeting.
- **Booking:** tidycal link on burtucala.com

### Step 2: Paid Strategy Meeting
- **Duration:** 60-90 minutes
- **Format:** Video (250) or In-Person (350)
- **Purpose:** Deep dive into the client's situation. Gather enough to produce a written output.
- **Preparation:** Client fills a brief questionnaire before the meeting.

### Step 3: Written Output
- **Length:** 3-5 pages
- **Delivery:** 3-5 business days after the Strategy Meeting
- **Content:** Situation summary, recommended path, next steps, relevant partner introductions.
- **Note:** This written output is always delivered, regardless of which service (if any) the client pursues next.

**For:** Operator-founders who want to build alongside an experienced team.

| Sprint | Duration | Price | Focus |

| Sprint 1: Architecture | 4 weeks | 2,400 | Venture design, space program, licensing plan, capex budget |
| Sprint 2: Execution Planning | 4 weeks | 2,400 | Critical path, contractor SOW, FF&E, marketing launch |
| Sprint 3: Pre-Launch | 4 weeks | 2,400 | Systems setup, team onboarding, soft-launch, KPI dashboard |
| Run Support (optional) | 4-week blocks | 800/block | Post-launch ops support, KPI review, SOP refinement |

**Total (full build):** 2,800 + (3 x 2,400) + optional run support = 10,000 base
**Payment:** Per sprint, upfront. Refund gate: if the client is not satisfied after Sprint 0, they don't continue.

**Ideal Client:** Operator-founder with a concept, needs structured execution support.
