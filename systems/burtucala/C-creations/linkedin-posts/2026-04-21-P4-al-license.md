14 steps, not 6. That is the real AL license application in Portugal.

The government website lists about 6. They skip condominium approval, Segurança Social registration, SEF guest reporting setup, the complaints book, and the insurance requirement.

3 tips that save weeks:

1. Start licensing before renovation is complete. Steps 1 through 5 can run while construction is underway. This saves 2 to 3 months on total project time.

2. Keep every protocol number. Every government submission generates one. You will need them all for follow-ups.

3. Do not assume your lawyer knows the current rules. AL regulations change frequently. Verify with the Camara directly.

Budget 2 to 4 months total. Fast municipalities: 2 months. Slow ones: 4+. Add 30% contingency.
